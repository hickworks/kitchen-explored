## Differences between Ninja OL501 vs OL601

-   **Capacity –** The Ninja OL501 has a 6.5 qt cooking pot and a 4.6 qt cook and crisp basket. On the other hand, the OL601 is built with a larger capacity of 8 qt cooking pot and a 5 qt cook and crisp basket. Visibly, this shows that the OL601 can handle a larger capacity than the OL501.
-   **Power –** The Ninja OL501 has 1460 watts while the OL601 has 1760 watts. This means that the OL601 is more powerful and hence can cook food faster than the OL501.
-   **Price –** The cost of purchasing the OL601 is higher with a price tag of $149.99 while the OL501 is lower with a price tag of $129.99.
-   **Dimensions –** The OL501 has a dimension of 14.21"D x 15.39"W x 13.07"H while the OL601 has 14.21"D x 15.39" W x 14.25"H.
-   **Weight –** The OL501 has a light weight of 24.03 Pounds while the OL601 is heavier at 25.1 Pounds.
