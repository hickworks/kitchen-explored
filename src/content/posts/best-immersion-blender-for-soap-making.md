What is an immersion blender? An immersion blender, also known as a hand blender or stick blender is a kitchen appliance that is handheld and used for operations such as making soap and also preparing food. It is equipped with a long shaft with a blade. It also has a motor that spins the blade on the other end.

It is very easy to use because all you have to do is submerge the blade into the soap or food to be blended. The power of the motors has the capability of delivering smooth and consistent textures to your preference. They are also easy to clean after use.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**What to use an immersion blender for?**

An immersion blender can be used for making smoothies, milkshakes, mayonnaise, pureeing baby food, mixing marinade, making soap and even crushing ice. Though the task of the blending operation high depends on the power of the motor. The more powerful the motor is, the more convenient results you achieve.

**How to clean immersion blender?**

-   The process of cleaning a blender is very easy. Follow the following steps:
-   The first step is to unplug the blender from the power source.
-   The second step is to disassemble the attachments or accessories if possible
-   The next step is to fill the brown with soapy and warm.
-   After this step, place the shaft and blades in warm water and soap, be careful not to place the motor or power cord in water.
-   After this, take a soft sponge or brush which you will use to gently scrub the bade and also the shaft
-   After finishing, rinse the blender with running to remove the formed residue.
-   The next step is to dry the blender thoroughly using a dry and clean towel.
-   Store the immersion blender in a cool and dry.
