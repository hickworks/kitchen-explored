## Top Soup Making Tips for the Vitamix

When it comes to making hot soup in a Vitamix, there are a few things to keep in mind:

-   First, ensure that you have put the Vitamix blender to correct settings.
-   Second, use the right ingredients.
-   The settings on your Vitamix will determine how smooth or chunky your soup will be. If you want a smoother soup, use a higher setting. If you prefer a chunkier soup, you will have to use lower settings. You can also pulse the soup if you want have more control over the texture to be produced.
-   As for ingredients, it's important to use fresh vegetables and fruits. Frozen vegetables can also be used though they do not produce a smooth soup. It's also important to use enough liquid. The Vitamix will extracts water from the vegetables as it blends them, so adding little liquid will produce a very thick soup.

Finally, here are some general tips for making great soup in your Vitamix:

-   Do not overfill the Vitamix blender. Soup usually expands while it's being blended, so leave some significant room at the top of the pitcher for soup expansion .
-   To make it very quick, you can start with a warm liquid to speed up the speed of heating up the soup.
