### Cleaning and storage recommendations

-   **Cleaning:**  Corelle dinnerware can be cleaned using a hand or a dishwasher. If you choose to use your hands, ensure to use a soft detergent and a soft sponge to prevent any scratches. This means you should not use an abrasive cleaner or steel wool. Using harsh detergents on the other hand may damage or destroy the pattern.
-   **Storing**: This dinnerware is easy to store since it can be stacked which saves on space. When storing them this way, ensure you do it carefully to prevent any scratches or chipping. Ensure you store the dinnerware sets in a dry and place that is free from moisture.

### Proper Handling Use

-   **Avoid hitting or dropping the sets:**  Corelle is known to be durable, though it's important to know it can also be vulnerable to breakage when dropped or hit by a hard object.
-   **Avoid sudden temperature changes**: Sudden temperature changes can cause the dinnerware to crack or break.
