## Ninja BN601

The Ninja BN601 is a powerful food processor that is very popular on the market. The BN601 is built with a 1000-peak motor which has the capability to blend even tougher ingredients with minimal effort. To add on top, it comes with a capacity under 11 cups, which has a combination of rated watt.

When it comes to performance, this blender is designed to handle professional tasks. This is because it is equipped with dedicated blades and discs that deliver consistent results. This means that you will be able to carry out precise chopping, uniform slicing as well as thorough mixing. Due to its versatility, it can carry out all these tasks effortlessly.

The other notable feature of the appliance is its 4 auto IQ intelligent programs and 3-speed settings. The 4 IQ intelligence settings are chop, dough, disc and more. The 3 speed settings on the other hand include low, high and pulse. All these features can be accessed and used with a touch of a button. You will have an easier time chopping vegetables, doughing, shredding cheese and more.

When it comes to capacity, the BN601 does not disappoint at all, it has a 9-cup or 72-ounce bowl. The bowl ensures that you are able to receive consistent results when carrying out small batches or large batches for guests or a large family. To add on top, the dough mixer is powerful and hence capable of folding ingredients evenly and quickly. This means it can dough a 2 lbs. in 30 seconds.

To enhance your culinary exploration, this processor comes with a 20-recipe guide. A guide that offers you ideas and guidelines for creating delicious meals and drinks. Cleaning the BN601 is nothing to worry about because it is equipped with dishwasher-safe components. The components of the appliance are also BPA-free, making them perfect and safe to prepare meals. Finally, the process of assembling the appliance is very easy and quick.

The appliance comes with the following:

-   1000-watt motor base
-   Precision Processor bowl equipped with a feed chute and a pusher
-   Quad Chopping blade
-   Dough blade
-   Reversible slicing
-   Shredding disc
-   20 recipe inspiration guide

### **Pros**

-   The Ninja BN601 is easy to use.
-   It is easy and quick to assemble.
-   It can also handle both small and large batches.
-   The BN601 is also BPA-free.

### **Cons**

-   The appliance requires proper usage and maintenance to last longer.

-   **Speed settings –** The BN601 is equipped with 3 speeds which are low, high and pulse. On the other hand, the BN600 does not have any speed settings. The speed settings enable you to choose the speed of your preference to carry out tasks.
