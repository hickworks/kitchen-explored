## Overview

The Ninja DZ201 offers two independent baskets while the AF161 gives you one large 5.5-quart basket. Both are among the best air fryers in their class.

## Key Differences

| Feature | DZ201 | AF161 |
|---|---|---|
| Baskets | 2 × 4 qt | 1 × 5.5 qt |
| Total capacity | 8 qt | 5.5 qt |
| Smart Finish | Yes | No |
| Price | ~$180 | ~$130 |

## Verdict

The DZ201's dual-zone cooking is a genuine game changer for families — cook two foods at different temps simultaneously. The AF161 is better for individuals or couples.
