## Vitamix E520

The Vitamix E520 is a high-performance blender that is powerful, durable and versatile. The blender is suitable for carrying out tasks in a professional setting. This blender features a 2.2 horsepower motor that is powerful enough to carry out blending operations with ease, including tougher ingredients. You will be able to crush ice, blend frozen fruits and more using this blender.

The other notable feature of this blender is its sleek and modern design that has the ability to enhance, any kitchen décor. Additionally, the blender comes with a 64-ounce tritan container which is perfect for large batch tasks. The container is also BPA making it very safe in food and drink preparations.

To enhance the functionality of blending, the blender comes with a tamper tool to assist you in pushing ingredients towards the blender blades. This tool is important because it prevents accidents if you use your hands to reach the blades. Besides this, the Vitamix E520 is equipped with 10 variable speed settings as well as a pulse function. Variable speed settings enable you to have full control over your blend speeds. The pulse function on the other hand allows you to customize your textures easily.

The other unique feature of this blender is 3 program settings. This program's settings are smoothies, hot soups and frozen desserts. Such a feature allows you to achieve precise blend textures without guesswork. Without forgetting, the short bursts of the pulse functions enable you to chunk salsas easily.

For lazy people, cleaning this blender is a simple task because it is equipped with dishwasher-safe parts. In addition, the versatility of this blender allows you to carry out a range of blend tasks. The blender also has an easier-to-use interface for both beginners to professional-level users.

The blender comes with the following:

-   Motor Base
-   64-ounce low-profile container
-   2 tumbler cups
-   Low profile tamper
-   Cookbook

### **Pros**

-   This blender is versatile.
-   It is also built from high-quality materials that make it durable.
-   The blender is perfect for large batch operations.
-   It is also easy to operate the blender.

### **Cons**

-   Some blend operations may be quite loud.

## Differences

-   **Motor power –** The Vitamix E520 has a more powerful motor with a power of 2.2 horsepower. On the other hand, the Vitamix 5200 is equipped with a 2.0 peak horsepower motor. From this statement, the E520 can blend tougher ingredients with ease compared to the 5200.
-   **Program settings –** The Vitamix E520 comes with 3 program settings which are smoothies, hot soups and frozen fruits. The Vitamix 5200 does not have this feature.
-   **Cost –** The cost of purchasing the 5200 is higher with a price tag of $468.92 while the E520 is lower with a price tag of $350.
-   **Cord length –** The 5200 blender has a cord length of 5.9 ft while the E520 has a longer cord length of 6.2 ft.
-   **Noise level -**The E520 has a higher noise level of 100.7 dB while the 5200 has a lower noise level of 96.6 dB.
-   **Dimensions** – The Vitamix E520 has a dimension of H- 17”x W – 7.7” x D – 9.4” while the 5200 has 7.25"D x 8.75" W x 20.5"H.
-   **Weight** – The E520 has a lighter weight of 10.1 lbs while the 5200 is heavier with 11.2 lbs.
