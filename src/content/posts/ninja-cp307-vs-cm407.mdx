## Ninja CM407 vs CP307

What are the differences and similarities between the Ninja CP307 vs CM407? These 2 amazing coffee makers are from the Ninja brand. They are all equipped with a sleek design and a pack of features that enhance their functionality. We will be comparing their capabilities, performance as well and features to help you decide on one that suits your needs.

**The main difference between the Ninja CP307 and CM407 is the brewing styles. The Ninja CM407 is equipped with 4 brewing styles which are classic, rich, over-ice and specialty. On the other hand, the CP307 comes with 5 brewing styles which are classic, rich, over ice, cold brew and specialty. The CP307 is not equipped with the specialty brew.**

## Ninja CP307

The Ninja CP307 is a hot and cold appliance that is versatile thereby making it convenient to prepare both coffee and tea. The appliance also can brew over ice, thereby enabling you to experience a smooth and sweet natural flavor in less than 15 minutes. It is also equipped with a user-friendly design such as separate baskets. The separate baskets allow you to brew coffee and tea which prevents mixing of flavors.

The other notable feature of the Ninja CP307 is the smart basket recognition. This feature can recognize a basket and display coffee or tea accordingly. The other amazing feature is its 6 different brewing sizes which enable you to carry out a single cup up to XL and full carafe. In addition to this, it comes with 5 brewing styles which are classic, rich, over ice, specialty and cold brew. This enables you to easily customize your coffee or tea.

The appliance also comes with a 50-ounce 10-cup carafe thermal carafe that is doubled-walled, making it capable of keeping your brew hot or warm for up to 2 hours. Additionally, a fold-away frother makes it easy for you to change or transform milky into a silky-smooth froth in a few seconds. Cleaning the CP307 is also an easy task because it is equipped with dishwasher-safe parts, including the water reservoir, whisk and more.

The appliance comes with:

-   50-ounce thermal carafe,
-   Separate brew basket
-   Permanent filter
-   Removable water reservoir
-   Ninja smart scoop
-   Fold-away frother

### **Pros**

-   The appliance is capable of maintaining your brew warm up to 2 hours.
-   A separate brew basket prevents the mixing of flavors.
-   The appliance can transform milk into a silky smooth frother in seconds.

### **Cons**

-   A few people claim that the purchasing cost is a bit high.

## Ninja CM407

The Ninja CM407 is one of the popular coffee makers that is highly loved on the market. The appliance is equipped with 4 brewing styles which are classic, rich, over ice and specialty. Specialty brew feature is known for producing super-rich coffee concentrate that is great for drinks such as lattes, macchiatos and cappuccinos.

For people who prefer iced coffee, the Ninja CM407 can brew over ice with ease, enabling you to experience a flavorful and refreshed ice coffee that does not have any dilution. To add on top of this, it has 6 brew sizes from a single cup to a full carafe. The other amazing feature is that it does not require the use of pods. This means you will be able to enjoy your coffee without using reusable pods. In addition to this, the appliance also comes with a 40-ounce water reservoir.

Cleaning the appliance is also a breeze since it is built with removable and dishwasher-safe parts. The parts include a glass carafe, permanent filter, water reservoir and more. To add on top, the CM407 has an automatic shutoff which shuts down the appliance after 1 hour of inactivity. Lastly, the CM407 features a built-in frother.

The appliance comes with:

-   50-ounce glass carafe
-   Fold-away frother
-   Permanent filter
-   40-ounce removable water reservoir
-   Ninja Smart Scoop
-   A recipe guide
-   2.5 cord length

### **Pros**

-   The appliance is affordable.
-   It can brew over ice.
-   The CM407 is also durable.

### **Cons**

-   New users find it a bit complicated to operate the appliance.

## Differences

-   **Brew styles –** The Ninja CM407 is equipped with 4 brewing styles which are classic, rich, over ice and specialty. On the other hand, the CP307 comes with 5 brewing styles which are classic, rich, over ice, cold brew and specialty. The CP307 is not equipped with the specialty brew.
-   **Wattage –** The CP307 has a higher wattage of 1500 watts while the CM407 has 950 watts. This means that CP307 can brew coffee faster than the CM407.
-   **Tea brewing** – The Ninja CP307 can brew tea and coffee while the primary function of the CM401 is to brew coffee only.
-   **Separate basket** – The CP307 comes with a separate basket for coffee and tea and the system recognizes it automatically displaying each. On the other hand, the CM407 does not have this feature.
-   **Thermal carafe** – The CM401 comes with a 50-ounce glass carafe while the CP307 is equipped with a thermal carafe that is double-walled. The double carafe keeps your brewed coffee hot for up to 2 hours.
-   **Dimensions** – The CP307 has a dimension of 11.81 x 10.01 x 15 inches while the CM407 has 8.75"D x 11.99" W x 15.04"H.
-   **Weight** – The CM407 weighs 3.13 Pounds while the CP307 has 11.7 Pounds.
