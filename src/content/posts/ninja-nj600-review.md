## Care and Maintenance of Your Ninja NJ600

Under this section, we will look at the do's and don'ts when it comes to maintenance and care of the instrument. When you take good care of the instrument, be sure that the instrument will serve you for a long period of time. On the other hand if you handle the instrument carelessly, there will be a high possibility of the instrument breaking down.

Ensure the Ninja NJ600 is always clean. The pitcher, lid, and blade assembly should be washed after using with soap and warm water. The pitcher can also be put into the dishwasher (dishwasher safe) for convenience. Ensure all the parts are dry before assembling them back.

The Ninja NJ600 base should be wiped down with a damp cloth after each operation. Never immerse the base in water or other liquids because this water can damage the motor.

To prolong the life of your Ninja NJ600 and keep it running at smoothly, it is advisable that you perform deep clean of the instrument once every month. You can use the user manual to guide you on how to carry out the activity.
