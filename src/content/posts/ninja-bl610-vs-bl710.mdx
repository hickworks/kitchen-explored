The Ninja BL610 is not equipped with any attachments. Though you can purchase cups and containers that are compatible with the blender.

The Ninja BL610 pitcher does not fit with other blenders. It is highly recommended to use the pitcher that comes with the blender since using a different one may damage the motor.

**How to take apart ninja blender bl610?**

The following are steps for taking apart the Ninja BL610:

1.  Remove the blender from the power sockets.
2.  Remove the pitcher from its sitting.
3.  Turn the blender upside down and locate the blade assembly.
4.  Push down the center of the blade and disconnect it from the pitcher.
5.  After disassembling you can clean and even check the condition of the components.

**How many blades does Ninja bl610 come with?**

The Ninja BL610 is equipped with 3 blade assemblies that have different heights and functionality.
