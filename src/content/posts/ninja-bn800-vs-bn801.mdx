## Ninja BN801

The Ninja BN801 with an Auto IQ takes your blending tasks to another level. The appliance is equipped with a modern design and well-enhanced functionality. It is a powerhouse that is well-designed to meet the needs of any kitchen. To start us off, it is very powerful with 1400 watts of professional power and Ninja Total crushing blades. This means that it has the ability to crush tough ingredients with ease, such as ice, frozen fruits and other ingredients. The power ensures that you are able to achieve your desired consistency.

Additionally, the Ninja BN801 also comes with an 8-cup precision processor bowl. This bowl enables you to experience precision processing activities such as chopping, smooth puree creation and more. Using this bowl enables you to handle a wide range of food processing tasks, thereby enabling you to have an efficient and enjoyable experience.

Furthermore, the appliance is equipped with 5 versatile functions that enable you to experience an easier time creating smoothies, frozen nutrient extraction, dough and more. All of this can be accessed with a touch of a button. On the other hand, the intelligent Auto IQ technology prevents guesswork tasks by carrying out timed pulsing, blending as well and pausing patterns. This enables you to have a perfect blend of results every time.

There is also on-the-go convenience which has been made possible with the help of 2 24-ounce single-serve cups. They are also designed with spout lids. This makes it very easy for you to carry your delicious and notorious drinks with you everywhere. In addition, you are also able to mix dough in seconds which is carried out by a powerful dough mixer. It is equipped with a torque enough to mix any ingredients quickly and evenly. Capable of carrying out a 2 lbs dough in less than 30 seconds.  This feature makes carrying out tasks efficient.

To add on top, the appliance comes with blades that are suitable for any task, e.g. dough blade, chopping blade, pro extractor blade and stacked blade. You are not limited to any blend task. Apart from this, the Ninja BN801 is also constructed with a 72-ounce total crushing pitcher that is great for handling large batch operations.
