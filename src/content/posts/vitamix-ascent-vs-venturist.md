## **What is a blender?**

A blender is a kitchen appliance used to create smooth, textured, and volumetric textures by using a variety of techniques such as blending, chopping and more. The most common use for a blender is to make smoothies, milkshakes, or soups; however, other people also use blenders to create chopped salads, salsas, nut butters, baby food, and sauces.

Blenders typically have multiple blades that rotate at high speeds, allowing them to chop and grind food quickly and evenly.
