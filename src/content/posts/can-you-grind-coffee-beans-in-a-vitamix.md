## How to Grind Coffee Beans in Vitamix

The process to grind coffee beans can be easy or hard depending on whoever is carrying out the grinding task. Today I have a secret that will be of help to you when carrying out any operations for grinding coffee beans in a Vitamix.

Below are secrets to a successful grind process of the coffee beans:

-   The first step to make sure you have a dry container. A dry container is suitable for the task because it has the ability to withstand pressure from coffee beans compared to the wet container.
-   The second step is to start with small amounts of coffee beans as you increase you way up. Inserting too many coffee beans into the pitcher can clop the blades.
-   Put the coffee beans into the pitcher or container and secure the lid.
-   After securing the lid, press the variable speed setting. Ensure you start with slow speeds until 8 to ensure that the coffee beans are finely ground. It highly recommended you start with a slow speed as you increase.
-   Finally, ensure to stop the blender periodically so that you are able to monitor the consistency of the grind so that you may adjust accordingly when need arises.
-   With these tips in mind, grinding coffee beans in your Vitamix should be a breeze.

Once the beans are ground, you can remove the lid and pour them into your coffee maker or storage container. Clean out the container well afterwards so that grounds don't get stuck in the blades.
