## Vitamix E320 Reviews

The Vitamix E320 is a high-performance blender that is suitable for any professional setting as well as home usage. The blender features a 1500-watt motor that has the capability to blend the toughest ingredients with ease. Some of the tasks the blender carries out include blending, chopping, grinding, heating and emulsifying. This makes it a versatile appliance when it comes to blending.

To add on top, the Vitamix E320 has a 64-ounce pitcher that is large enough to carry out large batch blending operations for guests and even a large family. The same pitcher is made of a durable and scratch-resistant material known as Tritan. A pulse function, on the other hand, allows you to easily customize textures to your preference.

Additionally, the blender is also equipped with 10 variable speed settings that give you full control over your blending operations. The other outstanding feature is the durable stainless steel blades. The blades are strong and sturdy to blend tough ingredients easily and quickly. This will enable you to achieve smooth and creamy textures.

The Vitamix E320 also comes with a tamper tool that assists you in pushing ingredients onto the blade. It is much safer than using your hands because the blades can injure your hands. It is also easy to clean this blender because it is built with dishwasher-safe parts. To build confidence in you, the blender comes with a 7-year warranty. This shows that the blender is high quality and durable.
