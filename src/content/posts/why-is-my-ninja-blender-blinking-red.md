## Reasons why Ninja blenders blink red

There are many reasons why your Ninja blender blinks red. The following are some of the causes of the blender blinking red:

-   **Overheating** – When you use your blender for an extended period of time continuously, the blender will start to overheat hence triggering the blinking red light. The blender is well designed with a safety precaution that shuts down the blender to prevent damaging the blender.
-   **Power issues** – The other issue that causes the red light is power issues. The cause can be faulty power, a damaged cord or even a blown-up issue.
-   **Lid or Pitcher not properly installed** – If the pitcher is not properly installed, the red light will be triggered and won’t operate. This is also a safety feature that prevents the blender from getting damaged.
-   **Blade obstruction –** When the blade is obstructed, this distracts the motor from functioning which triggers the red light.
-   **Faulty motor –** When the blender has a faulty motor, the red light is triggered.

If you have tried solving using the above methods, then it well be important to consult the services of a professional.

## Troubleshooting tips

-   **Allow the blender to cool:** If you allow the blender to cool, this will prevent overheating issues that would damage the blender and even trigger the red light. Let the blender cool for around 10-15 minutes before continuing blending tasks.
-   **Check power the source:** Check the source power to ensure the outlet is working correctly. If it is not the issue, check the power cable if it is damaged. If both are not working you can try different outlets.
-   **Check the pitcher and lid:** If the blender pitcher and lid are checked will, the red won't blink because the blender will work properly. Ensure they are well secured.
-   **Check blade:** In case you see the red light; you can try checking the blade if it is obstructed. If it is, then you can remove and foods that are obstructing the blade from spinning. Once you have solved it, the red will won’t turn on.
-   **Clean the blender:** It is also important to clean the blender and its components like the blades, lid and pitcher. By cleaning you will be preventing the blinking of the red light.
