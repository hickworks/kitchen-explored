## Care instructions and maintenance of fable dinnerware

1.  Do not use harsh chemicals or abrasive cleaners because they can scratch or damage the surface of the sets.
2.  It is highly recommended to use your hands to wash the pieces, use a soft sponge or cloth when cleaning. On the other hand, you can also use a dishwasher, though you have to use gentle wash to avoid damaging the dinnerware sets.
3.  Handle the sets with care because they can break when dropped or hit by a hard object.
4.  It is also advisable to avoid sudden temperature changes on the dinnerware sets. This can cause cracks or damage to the pieces.
5.  Avoid soaking the pieces for too long since it can lead to glazing or fading.
6.  Finally, store the pieces in a dry and cool place. When storing, place the pieces gently so that they don’t scratch or chip during the process.
