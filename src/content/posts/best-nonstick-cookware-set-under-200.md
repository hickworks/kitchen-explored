## Frequently Asked Questions

**Which nonstick cookware is safe?**

A non-stick cookware is considered safe when the cookware is free from harmful substances or chemicals that wont release any toxic fumes when you are cooking. It is also important to follow the manufactures instruction because some can become unsafe when overheated or when scratched.

**How long does nonstick cookware last?**

The life span of a non-stick highly depends on the following factors: the quality of the cookware, care and maintenance, frequency of use, utensils and heat. This means a high quality and well used non stick cookware can last for a long time.

**What is pfoa free nonstick cookware?**

A PFOA free non stick cookware is a cookware that is free from a chemical known as perfluorooctanoic. This is a harmful chemical that has been confirmed to be a health hazard and causes diseases such as cancer, thyroid and more.

**How safe is ceramic nonstick cookware?**

Ceramic nonstick cookware is considered safe because they do not contain harmful chemical like PFOA and PTFE which may be harmful to your health. The coating are constructed from sand and clay which are natural material as well as eco-friendly to the environment.
