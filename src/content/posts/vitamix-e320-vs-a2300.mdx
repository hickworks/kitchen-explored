## Vitamix E320

The Vitamix E320 is a high-performance blender that is known for its exceptional performance, versatility and durability. The versatility of this blender enables you to blend a variety of tasks such as smoothies, hot soup and more. To add on top, it is built with a modern and elegant design capable of enhancing any kitchen countertop.

Additionally, a 2.2 horsepower motor can blend and pulverize both tough and soft ingredients with little effort. When we say tough ingredients, we refer to foods such as frozen ice, crush ice and more. Besides this, the motor is well-designed to handle tough tasks without losing power or getting spoiled. The blender is also easy to use which has been made possible with the help of its friendly interface.

The other notable feature of this blender is its 64-ounce tritan container. This size is great for blending medium and large batch operations. This means you can blend a large batch at once without running the cycle again. It has proven to be a perfect option when handling guests or even a large family. Without forgetting, the Tritan container is strong and sturdy enough to handle tough operations with ease.

10 variable speed settings on the other hand allow you to have full control over your blend speeds. This will allow you to achieve your preferred texture easily. On the other hand, the pulse feature gives you an easy time to customize your blend texture. Some of the tasks you can achieve using this blender include chopping, grinding, blending and dough creation.

To ensure your blend operations are effective and safe, a tamper tool is available to use. A tamper tool plays an important role in ensuring that you achieve uniform blends and also prevents accidents. Accidents are prevented because you will use the tamper tool to push ingredients toward the blender blades. If you don’t use a tamper, the blades can injure or cut your fingers.

The Vitamix E320 also comes with a cooling running motor and a drive system. These 2 features ensure that the motor does not overheat, especially when handling extended blend sessions. Dishwasher-safe parts on the other hand make cleaning the blender a breeze. Here you will be able to save the effort and time of cleaning this blender.

Finally, the Vitamix E320 comes with a 7-year warranty which creates confidence in purchasing this blender. This shows that the blender is built from high-quality materials that can last for many years.

### **Pros**

-   This blender comes with a 7-year warranty.
-   Dishwasher-safe parts make cleaning the blender easy.
-   The blender is well protected from overheating.
-   The Vitamix E320 is durable.

### **Cons**

-   Some blend sessions can be quite loud.

## Differences between Vitamix A2300 vs E320

-   **Wireless connectivity** – The Vitamix A2300 comes with wireless connectivity while the E320 does not have this feature.
-   **Digital timer** – The A2300 is equipped with a timer while the E320 does not have this feature. This means the A2300 will be able to show you the blend time preventing over-blending while E320 will depend, on your personal judgement.
-   **Self-cleaning feature** – The Vitamix E320 comes with a self-cleaning feature while the Vitamix A2300 is not equipped with this feature.
-   **Self-detect container** – The A2300 has a self-detect container technology while the E320 lacks this feature.
-   **Price** – The cost of purchasing the E320 is lower with a price tag of $386.00 while the A2300 is higher with a price tag of $499.95.
-   **Warranty** – The A2300 comes with a 10-year warranty while the E320 comes with a 7-year warranty.
-   **Series** – The Vitamix A2300 belongs to the Ascent while the E320 belongs to the Explorian series.
-   **Dimensions** – The E320 has a dimension of 9 x 8 x 18 inches while A2300 has 11 x 8 x 17 inches
-   **Weight** – The weight of the Vitamix E320 is 10.5 lbs. / 4.76 kg while the A2300 is 11.86 lbs/ 5.5 kg.
-   **Cord length** – The A2300 has a cord length of 4 ft while the E320 has 4.5 ft / 1.37 m.
