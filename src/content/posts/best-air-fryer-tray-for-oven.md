## What is an air fryer tray?

An air fryer tray can be defined as a special cooking tool or accessory that is well-designed to carry out air frying tasks. Most of them have a flat, perforated tray or basket design that fits in air fryer oven chambers. The trays have a design that enables them to allow hot air to circulate around food evenly ensuring a quick and even cooking. The results produced are usually crispy.

Most air fryers are nonstick which makes them easy to clean and even use. Some also require little or no oil when cooking hence providing a healthy and delicious meal. They are great for frying chicken wings, vegetables, French fries and more.

## Features for air fryer tray for oven

There are some key features you should consider before purchasing an air fryer for oven. They include:

-   **Size and Compatibility** – This is a key factor to consider. Purchase a tray that is well-designed to fit into your oven by considering the appropriate dimensions. This will allow the tray to fit in properly without any gaps or instability.
-   **Non**\-**Stick Coating** – A quality non-stick coating enables you to use little or no oil thereby enhancing a healthy life. A non-stick surface also makes cleaning a breeze.
-   **Durable construction** – A tray that is well constructed with sturdy materials has the ability to withstand high temperatures without experiencing any warping or bending when in use. Some of the popular materials are stainless steel and heavy-duty aluminum.
-   **Even distribution of heat** – Choose a tray that can transfer heat evenly so that you are able to cook your food evenly. Even the distribution of heat prevents the formation of hot or cold spots.
-   **Versatility** – If you are lucky enough, some air fryers come with accessories like racks to expand your cooking capabilities. This will also enable you to cook multiple meals at the same time.
-   **Ease of use** – Select a tray that is easy to use, insert and remove from the interior of the oven.
-   **Dishwasher** **safe** – Some trays can be washed by hand while others can be washed by a dishwasher. If it can be washed by a dishwasher, this will save time and effort for cleaning.
-   **Compatibility** – Make sure that the tray meets the specification for air frying operations. An air fryer should have slots or perforations that allow easier circulation of air through the oven thereby delivering a crispy texture.

## **Frequently Asked Questions**

-   **When to use crisper tray in air fryer?**

A crisper tray is used in an air fryer so that you are able to achieve a crisper texture on your meals. It is suitable when creating meals with a crispier exterior like French fries, chicken wings and more. You can also use them if you want to use little or no oil which promotes healthy living.

-   **How to clean air fryer oven tray?**

It is very easy to clean an air fryer oven tray. The first step is to unplug the oven and remove the tray. Once you have removed the food items, let it cool first. Once it has cooled, use a sponge or brush to remove stubborn stains. The next step is to dip the air fryer in warm water that has mild dish soap. Use a brush to remove the remaining residue or grease. You can also opt to use baking soda and water with a non-abrasive cleaner. Once you are done cleaning, rinse the tray and let it dry. After drying you can reassemble it back.

-   **When to use tray in air fryer?**

An air fryer is applicable when you want to achieve a crisper texture.
