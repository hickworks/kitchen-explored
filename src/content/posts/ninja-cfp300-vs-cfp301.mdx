## Ninja CFP301 vs CFP300

Would you like to know the difference between the Ninja CFP300 and CFP301? If yes, then you are reading the right guide because we will be comparing these 2 powerful appliances from Ninja Company. Comparing the 2 will help you decide on which one suits your needs perfectly.

Let’s get started,

**The main difference between the Ninja CFP300 and CFP301 is the independent hot water system.
