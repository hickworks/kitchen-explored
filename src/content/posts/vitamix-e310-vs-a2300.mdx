## Vitamix A2300 vs E310

The Vitamix A2300 and E310 are some of the popular blenders from the Vitamix brand. These 2 amazing blenders are built for power, durability and also enhance versatility. Reading this guide will enable you to see these 2 appliances' similarities as well as differences. Knowing each appliance's features, pros and cons enable you to select the right blender for your tasks.

**One of the major differences between the Vitamix E310 and Vitamix A2300 is the power of the motor. The Vitamix E310 is powerful with a motor of 1400 watts while the A2300 is equipped with a 1500 watt motor. From our perspective, the A2300 has more blending capabilities compared to the Vitamix E310.**

## Vitamix E310

The Vitamix E310 has exceptional attributes and robust power. When we speak of power, it has a 1400-watt motor, which delivers formidable power that can effortlessly conquer any tough ingredients with ease. The motor also delivers smooth and consistent blend results, thereby enhancing the creation of diverse recipes with minimal effort.

The other feature of the Vitamix E310 is 10 variable speed controls. This feature allows you to have precise dominion over your blend textures as well as uniformity. It enhances versatility and enables you to easily create velvety purees to well-textured salsas with finesse. This ensures you can achieve blend results to your desire.

On the other hand, the pulse function offers you burst blends, which allows you to customize your blends with ease. The pulse feature works well when it comes to chopping vegetables, creating chunk dips and more. Besides this, the blender comes with a 48-ounce pitcher that allows you to carry out small and medium blend operations.

The material of the pitcher is tritan. A material that is used because of its strength and ability to withstand rigorous blend operations. Its strength also makes it resistant to cracking or even breaking. When it comes to safety, you don't have to worry because it is BPA-free.

The blades of the blender are hardened stainless steel that is constructed from top-notch aircraft stainless steel. The stainless steel strength and design ensure that blending tasks are done with ease. In addition to this, a 4.5 cord enhances flexibility when it comes to blending in areas that are far from the power source.

The self-cleaning feature and dishwasher-safe parts reduce the time and energy applied when cleaning the Vitamix E310. Having a blender that has this feature will save you time and effort for cleaning. The radial cooling fan and thermal protection prevent your blender from overheating, especially when operating for extended periods.

### **Pros**

-   This blender is versatile.
-   The Vitamix E310 material construction as well as design enhance its durability.
-   The blender is very easy to operate.
-   It is also easy to clean the Vitamix E310.

### **Cons**

-   The noise level can be quite high when conducting some operations.

-   **Dishwasher Safe –** These 2 blenders have dishwasher safe parts which makes it easy to clean.

## Differences Vitamix A2300 vs E310

-   **Motor Power** – The Vitamix E310 blender comes with a 1400-watt motor while the A2300 has a 1500-watt motor. This means that the A2300 can blend tougher ingredients with more ease than the E310.
-   **Wireless connectivity** \- The A23000 has a wireless connectivity feature while the E310 does not come with this feature. This means that the E310 can connect to the Vitamix Blend App.
-   **Pitcher size** – The Vitamix E310 has a 48-ounce container which is smaller than the A2300 which has a 64-ounce pitcher. The A2300 can blend more than the E310 in 1 cycle.
-   **Digital timer** – The A2300 can show you blending times because it has a digital timer. On the other hand, the E310 does not have a digital timer.
-   **Self-cleaning feature** – Cleaning the E310 is easier than the A2300, this is because the E310 can self-clean itself.
-   **Price** – When it comes to the cost of buying, the A2300 has a price tag of $499.95 while the E310 has $349.95. We can see that the E310 is cheaper than the A2300.
-   **Noise level** – Noise level is also another difference, the E310 has a noise level of 97.9 dB, which is higher than the A2300 which has 94.9 dB.
-   **Dimensions** – The E310 has a dimension of 17.7” H x 7.9” W x 9.1 D while the A2300 has 17.0 H x 7.8 W x 10.8 W.
-   **Weight** – The Vitamix A2300 is heavier with a weight of 12.0 lbs / 5.4 kg while E310 is lighter with a weight of 10.6 lbs / 4.8 kg.
-   **Cord length** – The cord length of the E310 is 3.9 ft / 1.2 m while the cord length of the Vitamix A2300 is 4.1 ft / 1.2 m.

## Frequently Asked Questions

-   **Is Vitamix E310 powerful?**

Yes, the Vitamix E310 is a powerful blender. Its 1400 watt motor makes it to be powerful. With such a powerful motor carrying out any blend operation is not an issue.

-   **Does the Vitamix A2300 have programs?**

Yes, the Vitamix A2300 has 17 programs that you can access once you pair it with the Vitamix Perfect Blend App.

-   **Is the Vitamix E310 good for smoothies?**

Yes, the Vitamix E310 is great for smoothies because it has a powerful motor and hardened steel blades which ensure efficient blend results.

-   **How much horsepower does a Vitamix A2300 have?**

When it comes to horsepower motor, the A2300 has 2.22 horsepower.

-   **Is Vitamix A2300 compatible with food processor?**

The Vitamix A2300 is not compatible with a food processor. It can still conduct food processor operations, but don't expect to achieve consistent results similar to a food processor.
