## Blending Process and Nutrient Retention

Blending the bananas, the right way prevents the loss of nutrition. If you don’t carry the process keenly you may end up consuming a blend banana texture with little or no nutrients.

The following are ways you can carry out allowing for maximum nutrients.

1.  **Use of ripe bananas** – It is very easy to blend ripe bananas since they are softer making it very easy you blend. The blend time reduces the blending time thereby reducing the time for exposing nutrients to heat and also air.
2.  **Blend quickly** – Ensure to carry out the blending time as quickly or in the shortest time. This reduces the time nutrients are exposed to heat and oxygen.
3.  **Prevent excess heat** – Blending produces heat which can affect the nutrient level in a banana. To prevent excess heat, use low speeds and also avoid blending for longer periods of time.
4.  **Use little liquid** – Using excess liquid may dilute the banana texture. For this reason, use little liquid to avoid diluting the texture hence losing nutrients.
5.  **Consume immediately** – It is recommended to consume the blend immediately. This is because the texture will be exposed to air and light which may affect the nutrient level. To achieve the best out of the blend, consume immediately.
