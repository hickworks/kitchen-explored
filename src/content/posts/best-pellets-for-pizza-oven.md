## Benefits of using a Pellet for Pizza Oven

-   **Convenience –** Pellets have proven to be more convenient than traditional firewood. They are well packed which saves on space and are also easy to handle. To add on top, pellet ovens are equipped with a feeding system, which means you don’t have to tend to the fire because the pellets will be fed automatically into the chambers.
-   **Consistent heat –** The other advantage is that pellets enable consistent and controlled heat. Precise temperatures ensure a steady and even heating when creating pizzas.
-   **Clean burning –** Pellets burn and produce minimal smoke and ash which leads to reduced emission and low production of residue. This makes it easy to clean the oven and also retains a clean environment.
-   **Energy efficiency –** Pellets are well designed and hence are dense which enables them to have high energy content. In short, they have the ability to produce a significant amount of heat that leads to low consumption as well as cost.
-   **Versatility –** Apart from being used in cooking pizza ovens, pellets are versatile and can also be used in pellet-fired appliances like the boiler, pellet stoves and more. Its versatility enables it to be used in multiple heating as well as cooking applications.

## **Frequently Asked Questions**

-   **How long do pizza pellets last?**

The duration in which pizza pellets can last depends on the size of the pellet grill used, temperatures applied and also the amount of pellets used in a session. This means the duration will vary from one person to another.

-   **How long does a 9kg bag of wood pellets last?**

A 9 kg bag of wood will last depending on the following: the pellet grill size used, the temperatures, the cooking technique applied and finally the wood pellets used. We can say it can roughly last 10 to 20 hours when cooking continuously.

-   **How many kg of wood pellets for a pizza oven?**

It is highly recommended to use 1 to 2 kgs of wood pellets in a standard oven per session. Though the amount used can be affected by the size, temperature and duration taken to cook.

-   **Can you use charcoal in a pizza oven?**

Yes, you can use charcoal in a pizza oven. As we all know is that charcoal is a very popular source of fuel. Charcoal produces high heat and a smoky flavor that can elevate the taste of pizza.

-   **How do you make pellets last longer?**

You can make pellets last longer by using efficient temperatures, cleaning the fire area, measuring the ratio to be used and also reducing unnecessary pellet consumption.
