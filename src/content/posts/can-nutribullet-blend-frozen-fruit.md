## Mistakes to avoid when using Nutribullet to blend frozen fruit

Below are some of the mistakes you should avoid when preparing Frozen fruit in a Nutribullet blender:

-   Ensure you fill ingredients up to the fill line. Overfilling can make it hard for the motor to spin, which may damage it with time.
-   Do not thaw the food to be blended. Thawing can damage the blades or even strain the motor.
-   Avoid using hot liquids because nutribullet is not designed to handle hot ingredients or liquids.
-   You the pulse function to break the ice, frozen fruit and any other tough ingredients. This allows for an even blend and also prevents damage to blades.
-   Avoid blending for too long, because this can lead to the blender overheating.
