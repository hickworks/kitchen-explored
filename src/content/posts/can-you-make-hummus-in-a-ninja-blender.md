## What is Hummus?

Hummus is a delicious, nutrient-rich dip or spread made from ground chickpeas, tahini, olive oil, garlic, and lemon juice. It's a staple of Middle Eastern cuisine that's become popular around the world in recent years.

Hummus is high in protein and fiber, low in calories, and an excellent source of vitamins and minerals. It's also vegan, gluten-free, and can be easily made at home with a few simple ingredients.

## Can you make hummus in a Ninja Blender?

Yes, you can make hummus in a Ninja Blender. The process is fairly simple and doesn't take long. Here's what you need to do:

### Ingredients of the Hummus.

-   Chickpeas
-   Tahini
-   Garlic
-   Olive oil
-   Lemon juice
-   Salt
-   Ground cumin

1\. Peel and chop the garlic.

2\. Add the garlic, chickpeas, tahini, olive oil, lemon juice, salt, and cumin to the blender.

3\. Blend until smooth.

4\. Taste and adjust seasoning as necessary.

5\. Serve with pita bread or crackers.
