## Overview

Lodge and Le Creuset are the two most recommended cast iron brands, but they differ dramatically in price. Is Le Creuset's premium justified?

## Key Differences

| Feature | Lodge | Le Creuset |
|---|---|---|
| Price | ~$40 | ~$200+ |
| Surface finish | Rougher | Smoother |
| Enamel option | Some | Signature |
| Made in | USA | France |

## Verdict

Lodge performs nearly identically for everyday cooking at a fraction of the cost. Le Creuset is for those who want heirloom quality and the aesthetic — both are excellent.
