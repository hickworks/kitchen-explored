## [Vitamix 7500](https://www.amazon.com/Vitamix-Blender-Professional-Grade-Low-Profile-Container/dp/B00LQT6UB2?crid=186CRQLKFGUSN&keywords=vitamix%2B7500&qid=1694430432&sprefix=vitamix%2B7500%2Caps%2C322&sr=8-1-spons&sp_csd=d2lkZ2V0TmFtZT1zcF9hdGY&th=1&linkCode=sl1&tag=ke2024-20&linkId=9df6e63e5f611c492c2bb2f55d27ed33&language=en_US&ref_=as_li_ss_tl)

The Vitamix is a powerful blender that has a combination of high-performance features with a sleek modern design. This makes it a popular appliance among professionals and blender enthusiasts as well. It is equipped with robust power and intuitive controls that ensure you have exceptional blending operations.

The Vitamix has a 2.2 peak horsepower motor which has the capability to blend and pulverize tough ingredients with minimal effort. Whether you are carrying out crushing of ice, blending frozen fruits, blending vegetables and more. The motor will deliver enough power that will allow you to achieve smooth and consistent blend textures.

The other feature is its 64-ounce low-profile container. This container can fit easily in most cabinets which saves on space. To add on top, the container is easy to maintain and also BPA-free. Making it safe to use in meal preparations. In addition to this, the container design allows for efficient blending operations and easy pouring of blends.

The other standout feature is the 10-variable speed control, also known as the speed dial. These speeds allow you to easily fine-tune your blend operations. You can choose to use gentle or low speeds depending on the textures you want to achieve. Pulse function on the other hand allows you to achieve chunky salsas or precise chopping using quick bursts.

Cleaning the blender is also a breeze, this has been made possible with the help of a self-cleaning feature and dishwasher-safe parts. All you need to do is add soap and warm water and run the blender for less than 30 seconds. This blender is also built with sturdy construction and design made from high-quality materials that enhance its durability. Hardened stainless steel on the other hand ensures a precision blend for each and every operation.

Finally, this blender also comes with a cooling fan that prevents that blender from overheating, especially when used for extended sessions. It is also versatile enabling a wide range of blending tasks. This includes blending, chopping, grinding and more.

## Similarities

-   **Horsepower –** Both appliances are equipped with a 2.2-peak horsepower motor. The motor is powerful enough to handle tougher ingredients with ease. This includes blending frozen fruits, crushing ice and more.
-   **Variable speed controls** – These 2 blenders have 10 variable speed controls that enable you to have full control over your blending speeds so that you achieve your desired textures and consistency.
-   **Pulse feature** – Both models are equipped with a pulse function enabling you to customize your blends with ease.
-   **Container and material** – The Vitamix V1200 and 7500 have a 64-ounce low-profile container. Large batch operations can be done with ease, thereby saving time and also electricity.
-   **Blade type –** These 2 blades are well constructed with hardened stainless-steel blades.
-   **Self-cleaning function** – The cleaning process is effortless because they are equipped with a self-cleaning feature. All you have to do is add warm soapy water into the container and run the function for about 30 to 60 seconds.
-   **Versatile** – Both models have the capabilities to carry out a wide range of blend operations. It can make smoothies, baby food and more.
-   **BPA-free** - Both blenders are BPA-free since they do not contain any harmful chemicals in their build-up. Which makes it very safe to be used in blending food.
-   **Minimum RPM** – These 2 blenders have a minimum RPM of 1400.
-   **Motor power** – Both blenders have a motor power of 1440 watts.
-   **Cool running motor** – The V1200 and the 7500 are all equipped with a cool running motor.

## Differences

-   **Cord length –** The Vitamix 7500 has a blend length of 5.6 ft/1.7 m while the Vitamix V1200 has 4.1 ft/1.2 m.
-   **Maximum RPM** – The Vitamix 7500 has a maximum RPM of 22800 while the Vitamix V1200 has an RPM of 22500.
-   **Digital timer –** The Vitamix V1200 has a digital timer while the 7500 does not. A digital timer helps you view blending times easily so that you don't over-blend. In other words, it prevents guesswork tasks.
-   **Series –** The V1200 belongs to the Venturist while the 7500 belongs to the G-Series.
-   **Weight –** The Vitamix 7500 has a weight of 12.5 lbs while the v1200 has a weight of 19.4 lbs. This shows us that the Vitamix 7500 is more portable to carry than the V1200.
-   **Cost –** The cost of purchasing the 7500 is higher than the V1200. This means the V1200 is cheaper than the Vitamix 7500.
-   **Self-detect containers –** The Vitamix V1200 comes with a self dect technology for containers while the 7500 does not. This technology allows the blender to automatically adjust the blending operation depending on the cup used.
-   **Dimensions** – The Vitamix V1200 has a dimension of 8"D x 10.5" W x 17"H while the Vitamix 7500 has a dimension of 7.7"D x 17.5"W x 9.4"H.
