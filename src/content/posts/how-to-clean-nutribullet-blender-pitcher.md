## How to Clean the Nutribullet blender?

-   Ensure that the blender is disconnected from the power socket before starting.
-   Start by disassembling the blender pitcher. You'll need to remove the blade assembly and the rubber gasket.
-   Fill the pitcher with warm water and add a drop of dish soap.
-   Swirl the water around and then let it sit for a few minutes.
-   Use a soft sponge or brush to scrub the inside of the pitcher, being sure to reach all the nooks and crannies.
-   Rinse the pitcher well with warm water.
-   Repeat steps 2-4, if necessary, until the pitcher is clean.
-   Let the pitcher air dry or towel dry before using it again.
-   To clean the blade assembly, simply add it to the blender pitcher along with some warm water and dish soap. Let it soak for a few minutes, then scrub it clean with a sponge or brush.
-   Rinse the blade assembly well, then dry it off before reassembling your Nutribullet.

Once you’ve got the pitcher clean, it’s important to dry it thoroughly before storing it away. Moisture can lead to bacteria growth, so make sure to towel dry the inside and outside of the pitcher before putting it away.
