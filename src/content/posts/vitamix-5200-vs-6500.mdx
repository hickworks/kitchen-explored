## Vitamix 6500 vs 5200

What is the difference between the Vitamix 5200 vs 6500? When it comes to high-performance blenders, the Vitamix brand is renowned for manufacturing blenders that have exceptional blending capabilities. The Vitamix 5200 and 6500 are some of the popular brands from Vitamix. Both blenders are highly loved by health enthusiasts, chefs and smoothie aficionados too. Today we will be discussing these 2 brands deeply, from their features, performance, pros and cons as well. This will assist you to purchase one that meets your preferences and needs.

**The main difference between the Vitamix 5200 and 6500 is the motor power. The Vitamix 6500 has a more powerful motor of 1680 watts while the Vitamix 5200 has a 1380-watt motor. This means that the Vitamix 6500 can blend tougher ingredients with ease than the 5200 blenders.**
