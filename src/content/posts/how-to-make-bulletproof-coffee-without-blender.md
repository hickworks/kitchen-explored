## What is Bulletproof Coffee?

A bullet proof coffee is a style of coffee that contains high levels of fat and low levels of carbs. The bulletproof coffee was invented by Dave Asprey. Dave said the coffee has benefits for creating mental clarity and focus. This was based on his research.

The main ingredient for bulletproof coffee is butter. Butter is usually added to brew so as to create a creamy texture. Some people prefer to use coconut oil or MCT oil to their coffee. The oil increase the fat level even further.

## How to Make Bulletproof Coffee without a Blender

**French press**

Brew coffee using your preferred method. We recommend using a French press for the best flavor. When your coffee is brewing, put 1-2 tablespoons of butter or ghee in a small pan with low heat. After melting, put 1-2 tablespoons of Oil to the butter and whisk until it mixes well.

**Hand Mixer**

The other process for making a Bulletproof Coffee without a blender is a handmixer. First, brew your coffee using whatever method you prefer. The next step, add a grass-fed butter MCT oil and then unsalted butter.

During this step you can also add your favorite ingredients. Finally, use a hand mixer (or a whisk if you don't have one) to froth the coffee until it's light and fluffy. Enjoy.

**Mason Jar**

This is also another convenient method that can be used if you don’t have a blender. The first process is add butter and oil into your mason jar. The step that follows is to put the mason jar into the microwave until the mixture melts well. After that, put fresh brewed coffee and firmly secure the lid. After sealing the lid, shake the contents till they mix well.

This process may not be perfect as other methods.

**Coffee Grinder**

The first step is to grind your coffee beans. Ensure to use a coarse grind setting on your coffee grinder. The next step is to add 2 tablespoons of ground coffee to the coffee maker. After that, put 1 cup of filtered water into the coffee maker.

Place the pot on the stove and turn the heat up to medium heat. Allow the water to boil for a few minutes. Allow the pot sit for 1 minute before pouring into your mug.

When your coffee is brewing, take 1 tablespoon of butter or ghee and 1 tablespoon of coconut oil or MCT oil and put into you mug. Once your brewed coffee has cooled slightly, pour it over the fats in your mug and stir well until well mixed. From here you can enjoy your coffee.

In order to create a great Bulletproof Coffee without a blender, ensure you have the following items:

-   Coffee beans
-   Water
-   Filtered water
-   Coffee grinder
-   Measuring cups and spoons
-   Unsalted butter or ghee
-   Coconut oil or MCT oil
