## What utensils work best for stainless steel cookware?

Would you like to know the best stainless-steel cookware? If yes, you are reading the right article because this guide is equipped with comprehensive information that will be important to you. We have also listed some of the best utensils for stainless steel cookware, this includes their pros and cons. The list will allow you to make a decision for purchasing one for yourself.

The following are some of the highly used utensils with stainless steel cookware:

-   **Wood** – Wood is a popular utensil, they are durable, biodegradable and they don’t create scratches on the cookware. They are also comfortable and poor conductors of heat; hence you can’t get burnt.
-   **Bamboo** – Bamboo is a plant that grows naturally. They are very comfortable to use, durable, heat resistant and won’t create scratches to your steel cookware. To add on top, bamboo is resistant to water, odors as well as stains.
-   **Silicone** – Silicon is also another great material that is used. This utensil can be used up to 650 degrees Fahrenheit which shows they are heat resistant. Additionally, they are non-toxic and do not leach chemicals into food.
-   **Nylon or nylon** – These are one of the most affordable utensils and are readily available on the market. They are also dishwasher safe making them very easy to clean. Apart from this, some of the plastic or nylon can be dangerous due to melting when reaching certain temperatures. On the other hand, if you are not using BPA-free utensils, you may risk leaching chemicals into your food.
-   **Metal –** Metal is also a great choice because they are very durable utensils that can last you for a long time. They are dishwasher safe and also easy to clean. The only downside of the utensil is that it can cause scratches to the utensils.

## **Frequently Asked Questions**

**What utensils will not scratch stainless steel?**

The following utensils will not scratch your utensils, they include silicone, wood, nylon, plastic and some stainless steel. Some stainless-steel utensils have a non-coating surface that prevents scratching.

**Are silicone utensils safe for stainless steel cookware?**

Yes, silicone is safe to use with stainless steel cookware. These utensils are heat resistant, which prevents them from melting or warping when used under high temperatures. To add on top, they are light and also easy to handle during cooking operations.

**Can I use plastic utensils on stainless steel cookware?**

Yes, plastic utensils can be used on stainless steel cookware. Plastic is popular because it is cheap, light and also safe to use on stainless steel cookware. The only downside is that they melt when exposed to high temperatures. It’s important to maintain the optimal temperature to prevent it from melting.

**Are nylon utensils safe for stainless steel cookware?**

Yes, nylon utensils are very safe to use on stainless steel cookware. This is because nylon is non-reactive as well as non-abrasive and it can’t scratch the surface of stainless steel cookware.
