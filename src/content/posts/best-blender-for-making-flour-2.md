## **What to consider when purchasing a flour blender?**

-   **Power** \- Blending flour requires a blender with a powerful motor. A powerful motor will enable you to achieve consistent results.
-   **Blades** \- You will also need strong blades to blend and crush flour gains. If the blades are not strong enough they can end up breaking or get damaged.
-   **Speed** \- A blender with a high speed has the ability to blend in a short period of time which saves blending time. Choosing a blend with a speed setting is something you should consider.
-   **Price** \- Choose a blender that fits within your means. Blenders come in a variety of brands with different price ranges. This means you can't miss a blender that fits within your budget.
-   **Pitcher size** - The size of the pitcher also plays a huge role. If you have a large family or usually have a number of guests, then getting a 72-ounce pitcher blender will be great. In case you are not many, a 64-ounce and below will be applicable.
-   **Cleaning** \- Cleaning a blender is a hard task for many people. Luckily, some blenders come with an inbuilt self-cleaning function. All you need is to add water and soap to the pitcher and let the blender clean itself for around 30 to 60 seconds.
