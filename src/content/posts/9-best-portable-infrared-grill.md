## What is a portable infrared grill?

A portable infrared grill is an outdoor cooking appliance that is equipped with infrared heating technology to carry out cooking operations. These grills are not like traditional grills that rely on direct heat from flames or coals to cook food. Instead, it uses infrared radiation to heat food. The process is usually faster and provides an even cooking.

## Features of a portable infrared grill

Below are some of the major features of a portable infrared grill:

-   **Infrared heating technology –** Just as the name says, any portable infrared grill uses infrared radiation technology to heat food and the cooking process is faster and even.
-   **Compact and lightweight** – These grills are constructed with a compact design and are lightweight which makes it easy you transport them from one outdoor setting to another like camping, trips, picnics and more.
-   **Adjustable heat setting** – The majority of the portable infrared grill comes with an adjustable heat setting, which allows you to easily control the temperatures of different meals to your preference.
-   **Quick ignition system** – These grills also come with a quick ignition system which makes it very simple to start the grill thereby cooking in time.
-   **Removable grates** – This feature makes it easy to clean the grill after cooking operations as well as maintaining it.
-   **Portable and versatile** – Portable infrared grills are versatile and can be used in a variety of outdoor settings, thereby making them suitable for a number of outdoor cooking operations.
-   **Fuel options** – These grills can use different fuel types like propane or even natural gas. This offers a flexible fuel choice depending on your needs as well as availability.
-   **Heat distribution** – Heat is evenly distributed hence ensuring food is cooked evenly.
-   **Searing capability** – Portable infrared have proven to be an excellent appliance for searing meat quickly using high temperature. This enables you to have a delicious crust while still retaining the tender and juicy properties of the meat.
-   **Durable –** They are also durable because they are built from high-quality materials that have the ability to withstand outdoor conditions.

## Frequently Asked Questions

-   **Do infrared grills really work?**

Yes, infrared grills do work. Infrared grills are equipped with a unique technology than the traditional ones. They use a radiant heat source other than heating and circulating hot air around the food. They can either use a ceramic or stainless-steel plate to transmit heat to the food.

-   **Can you boil water on an infrared grill?**

No, boiling water is not recommended on an infrared grill. It is important to know that infrared grills are designed to transmit high heat and direct heat cooking which is ideal for searing as well as grilling.

-   **Is infrared grilling healthy?**

Yes, infrared grilling is healthy. However, healthiness highly depends on the ingredients and techniques you have chosen for cooking.

-   **Is infrared safer than microwave?**

Both methods are considered safe depending on the purpose intended for. Both methods are safe when you follow the safety guidelines.
