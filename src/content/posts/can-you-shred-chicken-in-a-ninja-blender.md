## The following are easy ways to shred chicken.

**Shredding chicken using hands** – This one of the easiest and does not cost at all because you will use your hands to pull the chicken breast apart to your desired pieces sizes. Ensure you wash your hands before carrying out this process. The operation can be time consuming, and also highly depends on how fast you are carrying out the task. The faster you do it the lesser time you take to finish and the slower you are, the more time you take.

**Use of forks** – This is also another cost-efficient process. The only tools you require are 2 forks. To make this process easier for you, ensure that you boil your chicken to make it at least softer. Place your chicken on a sturdy. Put the 2 forks at the center or any position on the chicken and pull apart. It does not have to be the center; you can start from any position of your preference. Carry out the process of tearing the pieces apart until you achieve smaller and even pieces.

**Hand mixer** – This is also another process that can be used to shred chicken. This process requires boneless breast and thighs. Place the chicken at the center the bowl, ensure the bowl is fairly deep. Take the hand mixer and ensure you use low speed to avoid breaking the chicken apart.

**Stand mixer** – Stand mixer also shreds chicken very well. Just like hand mixer, this process works well with boneless breast and thighs. Take you chicken and put into the bowl of the stand mixer. Ensure that you apply the paddle attachment. The stand mixer should be on low speed to avoid breaking the pieces. You can carry out this process for 15 to 20 minutes until the chicken is shredded to your desired size.
