## How to shred cabbage in food processor?

1.  **Prepare the cabbage** – The first process is to remove any wilted leaves the cabbage has. Once you are done, rinse the cabbage with clean water so that any dirt or debris is removed. Don’t forget to cut the tough core which is located at the bottom of the cabbage.
2.  **Cut into pieces** – This step is for cutting the cabbage into smaller pieces that can fit into the food processor's tube. This will help the shredding process to be smooth.
3.  **Prepare the food processor** -Attach the attachment to your food processor base and ensure it is safely secured. If you are not sure you can refer to your manufacturer’s instructions.
4.  **Shredding** - Switch in the food processor and feed the pieces of the cabbage into the feed tube. During the process, you can use a food pusher to assist you push the pieces down to the shredding blades. This will prevent you from coming into contact with the blades. Put all the pieces until they are all shredded.
5.  **Collected shredded cabbage –** Once the pieces are shredded, switch off the blender and remove the attachment and put the shredded pieces into a container.
6.  **Repeat –** If the pieces are not satisfying, you can repeat the process until you achieve your desired shreds.
7.  **Clean the food processor –** It is highly recommended to clean your food processor immediately after you complete use. This will maintain your food processor and also prevent bacteria growth.

## Cleaning and maintaining the food processor:

-   **Unplug the food processor –** Before starting any cleaning process, it is very important to unplug the food processor from the power socket to prevent any electric accidents.
-   **Disassemble** – For this step, disassemble the parts of the food processor. This involves removing parts such as lids, blades, bowl and any attachments the food processor has. It is also recommended to refer to the manufactures manual if you are not sure how to disassemble, this will prevent you from damaging the blender.
-   **Wash** – Some parts of the food processor can be hand washed while others can be washed in a dishwasher depending on the food processor you are using, for hand wash use warm water soapy water. Ensure to use a gentle detergent and a non-abrasive sponge. Do not use abrasives because they can cause scratches and even damage the parts.
-   **Blade cleaning** – Use a brush to clean the blade. Avoid coming into contact with the blades because their sharpness can cut your fingers.
-   **Rinse and dry** – After washing, especially by hand, rinse the part to remove the soap residue. You can dry the parts before assembling the back. Drying prevents issues that may be caused by moisture.
-   **Clean motor base** – Use a damp cloth or a soft sponge to clean the motor base and also the main body. This will help clean up any residue, spills and splatters. Point to note, do not put or immerse the motor base in the water because water will damage the electrical components in the food processor.
-   **Storage** – Once you are using and cleaning, the best place to store the food processor is in a dry and clean place.

## Frequently Asked Questions

-   **How do you shred vegetables quickly?**

You can shred a cabbage quickly depending on when using a food processor. A food processor has the ability to shred quickly and consistently. The process can take less than 30 seconds.

-   **Which blade to use in food processor?**

A food processor blade to be used highly depends on the task you want to carry out. Some of the blades that come with a food process include an S-shaped blade, shredding disc, slicing disc, dough blade and citrus juicer attachment.

-   **How do you shred cabbage easily?**

You can shred cabbage easily by removing the outer leaves which are usually damaged or wilted. Cut the cabbage into half or quarters according to your preference. After you are done you can place it on a board and cut it into smaller pieces before placing it into a food processor.
