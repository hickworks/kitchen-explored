## Overview

Breville's Smart Oven lineup is widely regarded as the best countertop ovens you can buy. The Air adds air frying capability but costs significantly more.

## Key Differences

| Feature | Smart Oven | Smart Oven Air |
|---|---|---|
| Air fry function | No | Yes |
| Capacity | 0.8 cu ft | 1.0 cu ft |
| Heating elements | 9 | 13 |
| Price | ~$280 | ~$400 |

## Verdict

If you already own an air fryer, the base Smart Oven is exceptional. If you want one appliance to do it all, the Smart Oven Air justifies its premium with notably better results.
