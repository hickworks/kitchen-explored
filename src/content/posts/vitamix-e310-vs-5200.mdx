## [**Vitamix E310** Review](https://www.amazon.com/Vitamix-E310-Explorian-Professional-Grade-Container/dp/B07CVRZ1YJ?keywords=Vitamix+E310&qid=1687705715&sr=8-3&linkCode=sl1&tag=ke2024-20&linkId=251e6ed4127b30a9fc46382ce54a4cd8&language=en_US&ref_=as_li_ss_tl)

The Vitamix E310 is a powerful and compact blender that is very popular because of its high performance and also versatility. The blender belongs to the Explorian series, which targets producing high-quality blenders that are affordable compared to other blenders on the market. The blender features a 1380 watts motor that has the capability to blend tougher ingredients with minimal effort.

The Vitamix E310 is suitable for blending ingredients such as crushing ice, blending frozen fruits, pulverizing vegetables and more. It has the ability to deliver smooth and consistent results in a short time. On the other hand, it has a 48-ounce pitcher that is suitable for carrying out medium and small-batch operations. The container is great for couples, single individuals and even a small family.

The blender is very easy to handle and use since it has straightforward controls that are simple and also enhance a lot of functionality. The Vitamix E310 features 10 variable speed controls that enable you to easily adjust or change the speed of your blending operations. To add on top of this, the pulse function enables you to customize your textures easily.

The other notable feature of this blender is its versatility. It has the capability to handle a variety of blending tasks. This includes creating smoothies, pureeing, grinding nuts and more. This has been made possible because of its high-speed hardened stainless steel blades and motor. This ensures that you achieve efficient and consistent results on all your blends.

The blender is also equipped with a metal drive system and a cool running motor. The metal drive system ensures that your blender has maximum power when spinning while the cool running motor prevents overheating. On the other hand, the blender has a self-cleaning feature and dishwasher-safe parts which make the process of cleaning the blender a breeze.

When it comes to design, this Vitamix E310 is constructed with a sleek and compact design. Its appearance adds sophistication to any kitchen décor while its size makes you save on storage space. The parts are also BPA free meaning that it is safe to be used in food and drink preparations, does not contain chemicals. It also comes with a tamper that helps you push ingredients toward the motor. This prevents any accidents that would be caused by the motor.

### **Pros**

-   The Vitamix E310 is durable.
-   Its versatile functionality allows you to carry out a number of functions.
-   It is affordable.
-   Suitable for both small and large counter space.

### **Cons**

-   It can be quite noisy during operations.
