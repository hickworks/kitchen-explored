## Overview

Combo pressure cooker/air fryers have exploded in popularity. The Cosori Pro Gen 2 and Instant Pot Duo Crisp are the leading options in this category.

## Key Differences

| Feature | Cosori Pro Gen 2 | Duo Crisp |
|---|---|---|
| Capacity | 6 qt | 6 qt / 8 qt |
| Air fry lid | Built-in | Separate |
| App control | Yes | No |
| Price | ~$150 | ~$160 |

## Verdict

The Cosori's built-in air fry lid is more convenient. The Instant Pot Duo Crisp offers slightly better pressure cooking performance and the option to use the Duo lid alone.
