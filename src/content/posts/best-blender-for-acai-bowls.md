## **Frequently Asked Questions**

-   **What blender should i buy for acai bowls?**

-   **Do you need a blender for acai?**

Yes, you need a blender for acai. You need a powerful blender, at least 100 watts and above to carry out the operation effective. If you use a low powered blender or no blender, you may end up receiving inconsistent results which may not be great. For this reason, a blender is the best way to achieve consistent acai bowl resulst.

-   **Can you use a nutribullet for acai?**

No, nutribullet is not great to use for acai. As we have seen, to carry out acai operations use need a powerful blender with at least 1000 watts, below this the blend result may be ineffective and inconsistent. In other words, choose a powerful blender with 1000 watts and more to achieve consistent results.

-   **What liquid is best for acai?**

The best liquid for acai is nut milk, coconut water, fruit juice and water. The best liquid to use highly depends on your personal preferences as well as other ingredients. It is also great to try different liquids so that you can find one that suits you.

-   **Why is acai expensive?**

Acai is expensive for the following reasons:

-   Acai berries are labor intensive to harvest.
-   They grow in limited regions.
-   They also have a high demand.
-   They are also highly perishable.
