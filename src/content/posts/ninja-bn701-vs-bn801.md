## Overview

The Ninja BN701 and BN801 are both high-performance blenders in Ninja's Professional Plus line, but they differ in capacity and extras.

## Key Differences

| Feature | BN701 | BN801 |
|---|---|---|
| Capacity | 72 oz | 72 oz + 16 oz personal |
| Auto-iQ programs | 3 | 4 |
| Personal cup | No | Yes |
| Price | Lower | Higher |

## Verdict

If you regularly make single-serve smoothies, the BN801's personal cup makes it worth the upgrade. Otherwise the BN701 is an excellent stand-alone blender.
