## Overview

Moen is the go-to brand for reliable kitchen faucets. The Arbor and Spot Resist lines both offer excellent quality but differ in their finish technology and feature sets.

## Key Differences

| Feature | Arbor | Spot Resist |
|---|---|---|
| Finish protection | Standard | Spot-resist stainless |
| Pull-down spray | Yes | Yes |
| Reflex system | Yes | Yes |
| Price | Lower | Higher |

## Verdict

If fingerprints drive you crazy, Spot Resist is genuinely worth the premium — especially around kids. For others, the standard Arbor is a top-rated, reliable faucet at a better price.
