## Differences between Vitamix 750 vs A3500

-   **Touch Interface**: The Vitamix 750 blender has a manual control panel while the A3500 model has a touch screen control panel. The touch control panel is easier to use and also enhances the appearance of the blender.
-   **Programmed Timer**: The A3500 is equipped with a program timer while the Vitamix 750 is not. Viewing blending time is made easy using these features, it prevents guesswork tasks.
-   **Self-detect technology**: The A3500 model has self-detect technology, which automatically recognizes the size of the container you are using and adjusts the blending settings accordingly. The Vitamix 750 does not have the self-detect feature.
-   **Wireless connectivity**: The A3500 has wireless connectivity while the Vitamix 750 is not equipped the wireless feature have this feature. Such a feature assists you to easily connect the A3500 with any smart device.
-   **Price**: The A3500 blender has a higher purchasing price of $649.95 while the Vitamix 750 blender is relatively affordable with a price tag of $584.99.
-   **Cord length**: The cord length of the Vitamix A3500 is 3.2 ft or 1.2 m while the Vitamix 750 has a length of 6 ft. Lengths play a crucial role, especially when your power socket is located away from the countertop.
-   **Noise level**: The noise level for a Vitamix 750 is 98 dB while the Vitamix A3500 has a noise level of 92.8.
-   **Blending time indicator**: The A3500 enables you to monitor blend times easily, thereby preventing guesswork. On the other hand, the Vitamix 750 does not have this feature.
