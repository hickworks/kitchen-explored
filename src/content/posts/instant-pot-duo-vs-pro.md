## Overview

The Instant Pot Duo is the world's best-selling pressure cooker. The Pro adds more features and smarter cooking programs — but is the upgrade worth the extra cost?

## Key Differences

| Feature | Duo | Pro |
|---|---|---|
| Programs | 13 | 28 |
| Steam release | Manual | Quick/normal valve |
| Inner pot | Stainless | Stainless + nonstick |
| Price | ~$100 | ~$130 |

## Verdict

The Duo handles 95% of what most cooks need. The Pro's improved steam release valve and extra programs are appreciated but not essential unless you pressure cook daily.
