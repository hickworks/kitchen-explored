## Ninja CFP301

The Ninja CFP301 is an innovative appliance that has a seamless combination. It is versatile since it is able to brew both grounds and pods. The appliance comes with 9 ground sizes which range from a small cup carafe to a full cup carafe. To add on top of this, it also has 4 pod brew sizes hence diver your cravings.

Additionally, the appliance does not only offer you size only but also style too. The Ninja CFP301 comes with 4 brewing styles which are classic, rich, over ice and specialty. This enables you to experience a well-tailored coffee experience for your taste buds. When it comes to specialty feature, the appliance offers you supper-rich coffee concentrate. This is suitable for drinks such as lattes, macchiatos, cappuccinos and more. You will be able to enjoy a variety of drinks in your comfort at home.

When it comes to innovation, the dual brew is equipped with an independent hot water system that has 2 temperature controls, which are hot and boil. This also enhances the versatility of creating your coffee. Creating drinks such as soups, oatmeal, hot cocoa and more is an easy task for this appliance. For those who prefer frothy smooth milk, you are sorted because this dual brew has a fold-away frother to transform your milk.

The other notable feature of the Ninja CFP301 is the multi-position water reservoir. This reservoir can be replaced either at the side or rear of the machine depending on your counter space. To add on top, it is a 60-ounce reservoir that is easy to remove and refill in sinks. Without forgetting, it also hosts an independent hot water dispenser. Another amazing feature is the Thermal flavor extraction that ensures even saturation, and precise temperatures for both grounds and pods. The end result is ultra-flavorful coffee.

Maintaining this appliance is also easy. It is equipped with a cleaning function, dishwasher-safe components, removable parts and more which make it very easy to clean.

The appliance comes with the following:

-   Dual pro coffee system
-   Ninja Pod Adapter
-   12-cup glass carafe
-   Fold-away frother
-   Paper filter kit
-   Removable water reservoir equipped with an independent hot water dispenser.

### **Pros**

-   The appliance comes with a removable water reservoir equipped with an independent hot water dispenser.
-   It is also equipped with a fold-away frother.
-   Capable of handling up to 12-cup glass carafe.
-   It is also easy to clean.

### **Cons**

-   A few people may require a learning curve.

## Ninja CM401

The Ninja CM401 is another great coffee maker that you should consider. This coffee maker is equipped with 4 versatile brew styles which enable it to suit any preference. These styles include the classic, rich brew, over ice and specialty. Over iced brew is great for hot days, it is always flavorful and refreshing. The last is the specialty brew that offers supper-rich concentrate coffee. Just similar to the Ninja CFP301, great for styled drinks such as macchiatos, cappuccinos, lattes and more.

The other notable feature is its six brew sizes capable of handling all your needs. This ranges from a single cup to a full carafe. The sizes are a cup, XL cup, travel mug, multi-serve travel mug, half carafe and a full carafe. Thus, gives you the flexibility to brew your preferred brew-size coffee in regard to the occasion taking place.

Another standout feature of this appliance is that it does not require the use of pods. This means that you can create your coffee grounds to single-serve coffee without the use of pods. It allows you to enjoy your brewed coffee and at the same time eliminates the use of waste. Giving you the freedom to explore a number of flavors as well as roasts.

Cleaning is an easier task too because the Ninja CM401 has dishwasher-safe components and removable parts. This makes it very easy to clean and maintain the machine. To enhance convenience, the appliance features an automatic shutoff function that turns off the machine, after 1 hour of inactivity. This saves energy as well as peace of mind.

Finally, turning your milk into a silky smooth froth is possible with the help of an inbuilt fold-away frother. It is also easy to remove for cleaning. To add on top, it comes with a 40-ounce removable water reservoir that is easy to clean.

The appliance comes with a:

-   Ninja coffee maker
-   50-ounce or 10-cup glass carafe
-   Fold-away frother
-   Permanent filter
-   40-ounce removable water reservoir
-   Ninja smart scoop
-   Recipe guide
-   Quick guide
-   2.5 cord length

### **Pros**

-   The appliance is affordable.
-   It is also easy to use.
-   The machine is also built to last.
-   Cleaning is also a breeze.

## Differences

-   **Brew sizes** – The CFP301 has 9 brew sizes and 4 traditional pod brew sizes. On the other hand, the CM401 has 6 brew sizes.
-   **Water reservoir capacity** – The CFP301 is constructed with a 60-ounce water reservoir while the CM40 has a 40-ounce water reservoir.
-   **Multi-position water reservoir** – The CFP301 is equipped with a multi-position water reservoir and can be placed either on the side or rear. On the other hand, the CM401 is not equipped with this feature.
-   **Thermal flavor extraction** – The CFP301 is equipped with this feature while the CM401 does not.
-   **Pod compatibility** – The Ninja CFP301 uses a Ninja pod adapter for pods while the CM401 does not require the use of reusable pods.
-   **Automatic shut-off** – The CM401 is equipped with an automatic shut-off after an hour of inactivity while the Cfp301 does not have this feature.
-   **Cups –** The CFP301 can handle up to 12 cup glass carafe while the CM401 can handle up to 10 cup glass carafe.
-   **Price –** The cost of purchasing the CFP301 is higher with a price tag of $199.99 while the CM401 is lower with a price tag of $159.95.
-   **Dimensions –** The dimension of the CM401 is 11.99 x 8.75 x 15.04 inches while the CFP301 is 9.13"D x 11.39" W x 15.54"H.
