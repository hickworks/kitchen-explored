**Does all gold flatware fade?**

Yes and no, the fading of the gold flatware highly depends on the material's quality or construction and usage. If you have high-quality gold flatware and use it well it will not fade. On the other hand, if you a low quality and high-quality flatware and use it improperly, they will all fade.

**How do you keep gold silverware from tarnishing?**

To prevent the gold silverware from tarnishing, you should handwash, avoid using abrasives, store properly, use recommended cleaning solutions and store properly.
