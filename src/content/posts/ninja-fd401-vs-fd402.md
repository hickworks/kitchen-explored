## Overview

The Ninja FD401 and FD402 are both excellent multi-cookers, but they serve slightly different needs.

## Key Differences

| Feature | FD401 | FD402 |
|---|---|---|
| Capacity | 8 qt | 8 qt |
| Air Fryer Lid | Separate | Attached |
| Price | Lower | Higher |

## Verdict

If you want a cleaner countertop experience, the FD402's attached lid is worth the extra cost. For budget-conscious shoppers, the FD401 performs identically on cooking functions.
