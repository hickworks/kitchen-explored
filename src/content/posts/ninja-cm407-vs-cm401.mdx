## Ninja CM407

The Ninja CM407 is a versatile powerhouse that can enhance your brewing performance. The appliance is equipped with 6 different sizes that make it suitable for any occasion. This ranges from a single cup to a full carafe, it has you covered for any occasion. This is one of the reasons coffee lovers like it.

Apart from the brew size, the CM407 also offers a range of brew styles. These styles include classic, rich, over ice and specialty. With this feature, you will be able to enjoy classic black coffee, robust brew and also enjoy iced coffee and more. To add on top of this, it has a 50-ounce thermal carafe which ensures that the brew remains hot while, at the same time ensuring easy pouring.

The other stand-out feature of this appliance is the built-in fold-away frother. This makes it very simple for you to transform milk into a velvety froth. This has proven to be great for creating specialty drinks such as cappuccinos, macchiatos and more. With this feature, you will be able to have your favorite brew in the comfort of your home with minimal effort.

The CM407 does not require the use of pods, instead, it comes with a reusable filter and a measuring scoop enhancing its sustainability. It has a small size but plays a huge role in reducing waste. On the other hand, the thermal technology ensures that your experience is a flavorful delight.

The other feature is a removable water reservoir which is very easy to refill as well as timely nonfictions when cleaning. Using the appliance will enable you to experience efficiency and fun while creating your coffee. Finally, it has a sleek black stainless-steel design that adds a touch of elegance to any kitchen.

-   It does not require the use of pods.
-   Refilling is also easy.
-   Comes with 6 different sizes.

## Ninja CM401

The Ninja CM401 is also a great coffee maker that produces outstanding brewing results. The coffee maker comes with 4 versatile brewing styles perfect to meet your preferences. These styles include classic, rich, over ice and specialty brew. Over-ice brew is well designed for brewing coffee over ice, making it well flavored and does not have any dilution. Specialty, on the other hand, enables you to have a super-rich concentrated coffee. It is perfect for styled drinks such as lattes, macchiatos, cappuccinos and more.

Its versatility does not stop at the styles, the appliance also comes with 6 brew sizes. This size ranges from a single cup to a full carafe. This means you will be able to enjoy a quick cup of coffee on the go and even a large batch to enjoy with friends. It is capable of serving you well for any occasion.

To add on top of this, the appliance is easy to use as well as maintain. You can create coffee grounds without the need for reusable pods. In addition, it also has a built-in fold-away frother enabling you to turn milk to a silky smooth froth with minimal effort. Without forgetting, it comes with a removable water reservoir that is very easy to clean and refill.

Cleaning is also an easier operation because it is equipped with removable parts and dishwasher-safe components. Furthermore, it has an automatic shutoff feature, which turns off the appliance after one hour of inactivity. This gives you peace of mind and also saves energy.

The Ninja CM401 comes with:

-   50-ounce glass carafe
-   Fold-away frother
-   Permanent filter
-   40-ounce removable reservoir
-   Ninja Smart scoop
-   Recipe guide
-   2.5 cord length

## Differences

-   **Thermal carafe** – The Ninja CM401 has a 50-ounce glass carafe while the CM407 has a thermal carafe. This means that the CM407 is capable of retaining heat longer.
-   **Removable water reservoir capacity** – The CM401 has a removable water reservoir capacity of 40 ounce while the CM407 has a capacity of 50 ounce.
-   **Dimensions –** The CM401 has a dimension of 11.99 x 8.75 x 15.04 inches while the CM407 has 8.75"D x 11.99" W x 15.04"H.
