The blend results delivered are consistent and do not contain chunks or lumps.

Additionally, the blender also comes with 10 variable speed settings. This feature makes it easy for you to adjust the blending speeds so that you are able to achieve your preferred texture as well as consistency. You will be able to carry out slow or fast blends depending on what you are blending. This feature will allow you to tackle a range of recipes such as pureeing baby food or even creating chunky salsa with ease and precision.

The other notable feature is its 64-ounce container. This container enables you to blend larger batches which are suitable for a large family and even guests. The container is BPA-free and made from ABS plastic. The container is also strong and sturdy allowing it to withstand rigorous tasks every day. It also comes with a wide-shaped tamper that helps you to push ingredients toward the blades which prevents accidents.

Cleaning the Vitamix 5000 is also very easy because it is constructed with dishwasher-safe components. This will save you the effort and time of cleaning. The other standout feature is the stainless-steel hammermill wet blade which is strong enough to blend any operation. The Vitamix 5000 is also able to carry out small batch operations using the 32-ounce container.

## Vitamix 5200

The Vitamix 5200 is also a high-performance blender that is known for its durability, versatility as well functionality. This blender is great for both professional and home use. To start off, it features a 2-peak horsepower motor that has the ability to easily pulverize even tougher ingredients. It is constructed with stainless blades which have the capability to spin at high speeds and produce a vortex that ensures an efficient blending operation.

This blender is also equipped with 10 variable speeds enabling you to have full control over your blending speeds. As we have discussed earlier, these speeds allow you to easily achieve your preferred texture as well as consistency. On the other hand, a pulse function makes it easy for you to customize your blend textures. This makes it suitable to chop, grind and even create chunky textures.

The Vitamix 5200 also has a large container of 64 ounce or 2 liters. This is a large size enough to serve a number of guests and even a large family. The container is also BPA-free since it is free from chemicals such as PFOA and more. Its durability also ensures that you will be able to use this blender for a long time. This will be possible if you only maintain and use the blender properly.

This blender also comes with a self-cleaning feature and dishwasher-safe component, this feature makes cleaning the Vitamix 5000 a breeze. All you need is warm soapy water and run the blender for about 30 to 60 seconds. The self-cleaning feature has the ability to remove any food particles or residue stuck on the blades and container.

The other notable feature of this blender is its versatility. Using this blender will enable you to carry out a number of blend tasks such as pureeing, chopping, grinding and emulsifying. ## **Differences between the Vitamix 5000 vs 5200**

The following are the differences between the 5000 vs 5200 Vitamix:

-   **Availability** – The Vitamix 5200 is readily available on the market for purchase while the Vitamix 5000 is hard to find on the market because it is an older model.
-   **Cleaning** – Both blenders are dishwasher safe but the Vitamix 5200 has a cleaning feature while the 5000 does not. This means it is far easier to clean the 5200 than the 5000.
-   **Cost** – The cost of purchasing the 5200 is higher than the 5000. If you are on a budget, then the 5000 is a good choice but if you want a high-performance equipped with advanced features, then the 5200 is a good option.
-   **Noise level** – The Vitamix 5000 has a higher noise level than the 5200. This is because the 5200 has a quieter motor.
-   **Cooling motor –** The 5200 has a cooling motor while the 5000 does not. This means that the 5200 will not overheat when in use especially when blending tougher foods. On the other side, the 5000 is likely to overheat because it lacks this feature.
-   **Warranty –** The Vitamix 5200 has a 7-year warranty while the Vitamix 5000 comes with a 5-year warranty.
-   **Dimensions –** The Vitamix 5200 has a dimension of 7.25"D x 8.75" W x 20.5"H while the 5000 has a dimension of 8-3/4"L x 7-1/4"W x 8-3/4"H.
