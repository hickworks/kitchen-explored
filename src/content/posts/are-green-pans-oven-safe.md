## **Safety tips for using green pans in an oven**.

1.  First, check the maximum oven temperature the pan can handle before using it. Different green pans collections have different temperature ratings. Using the right temperature rating will prevent pans from damage.
2.  Avoid sudden temperature changes. Always allow the pans to reach room temperature before placing them in an oven or refrigerator. Sudden changes in temperature can cause damage to the pans.
3.  Use protective gear such as mitts or thick towels when using the oven. This will protect you from burns caused by the oven or the pan.
4.  Avoid using metal utensils or scrubbers because they can cause scratches and even damage the nonstick surface of the pan.
5.  Avoid placing your green pan on uneven surfaces because it causes warping or bends to the cookware.
