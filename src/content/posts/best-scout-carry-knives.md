## Frequently Asked Question

-   **What is the origin of scout carry knives?**

Scout carry knives was designed by Karl Elsener in 1891. He designed the knife to serve the needs of the Swiss army.

-   **How to scout carry a knife?**

A scout carry knife is designed to be worn horizontally on the belt. Most come with a sheath that is equipped with a loop or hooks to attach to the belt. This allows you to remove the knife easily and quickly.

-   **What is scout carry for a knife?**

Scout carry is a technique for carrying a knife on the belt while in a cross-draw or horizontal position. The knife handle is positioned in a way that allows you to easily access the knife using one hand.

-   **why a scout sheath for a knife is the best way to carry a knife?**

A scout sheat is the best way because of the following, the knife is well secured and can't cause any accidents. The second reason is that they are very comfortable to wear and also easy to access.
