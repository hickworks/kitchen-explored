## How Does the Air Fryer Work?

An air fryer is a kitchen appliance that operates by circulating hot foods. The air fryer is equipped with a heating element and a fan. Once you put your food in and turn on the air fryer, the air is heated and pushed down as hot air, which then circulates the food. The circulation of air makes the food crispy or has a fried texture.

The food is cooked in a convectional heating process, the process is very simple to understand since hot air moves around the food, thereby cooking it faster and more evenly than in a traditional oven. Air fryers are equipped with controls for temperature and time so that you can adjust the cooking process to your desired preferences. Once done, the air fryer automatically switches off because of the time you set.

## Health Risks Associated with Air Frying

Using air fryers can lead to the formation of acrylamide, which is a harmful chemical that is formed when various foods like potatoes are cooked at very high temperatures.

On top of this, some scientists have found that air frying can also lead to the formation of advanced glycation as well as heterocyclic amines.
