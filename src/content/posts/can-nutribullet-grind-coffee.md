**How to grind Coffee in a Nutribullet?**

This amazing instrument has the ability to quickly and easily grind coffee beans. The big question is how you can grind the coffee beans using the instrument.

Here are steps that you can use to grind coffee in a Nutribullet:

1.  The first step is to put coffee beans into the pitcher.
2.  Second step is to secure the blender lid till you here a click sound.
3.  The next step is to push the grind button to start the grinding operation.
4.  Ensure you hold the button for around 30 to 60 seconds until you achieve your desired texture.

## Types of Grinds that Nutribullet can produce.

Using the Nutribullet will enable you to achieve a variety of grinds, which are Coarse grind, medium, fine and extra fine grind. The consistency of the grind will highly depend on how you will brew the beans and also your preference.

The following are grind textures that you will be able to achieve:

**Coarse grind –** Nutribullet has the ability to produce coarse grind. To achieve this, ensure you put 3 quarters of the beans into the container and pulse 3 times. Ensure you monitor the process because you can over grind the beans when trying to achieve a coarse grind.

**Medium grind –** Medium grind is similar to pre-ground coffee, similar the one found at the store. This kind of grind is suitable for siphon brewers as well as drip coffee makers.

**Fine grind** – To achieve the fine grind, you will have to run the nutribullet for 1 to 2 minutes while having a 10 second interval stop.  Medium grinds are suitable for making espressos.

**Extra fine grind** – This type of grind is also referred to as “Turkish grind”. They are used in cezve coffee making. The grind has the consistency similar to the one of a powder sugar. To achieve this kind of texture, ensure the blender runs 10 to 15 seconds intervals.

## Cleaning Nutribullet after grinding Coffee beans.

Ensure to clean your Nutribullet blender after using in order to avoid any contamination and also interference with flavors.

1.  The first step is to disconnect the cup from the base. You can wash the cup with yous hands or a dishwasher. Incase the cup has a stains, pour warm water and soap into the cup and let soak for 5 to 10 minutes before washing again.
2.  Once you are done with the cup, clean the base using a damp cloth. You wipe the base carefully so that you don’t get cut by the blades. Ensure you wipe all the residue.
3.  After cleaning, its recommended that you give the Nutribullet blender a final rinse using clean water and then use a dry piece of cloth to wipe the instrument before storing.
