## Overview

Portable blenders have come a long way. The BlendJet 2 and NutriBullet Go are the top picks for travel, gym bags, and office desks.

## Key Differences

| Feature | BlendJet 2 | NutriBullet Go |
|---|---|---|
| Capacity | 16 oz | 24 oz |
| Battery life | ~15 blends | ~10 blends |
| Charge type | USB-C | USB-C |
| Price | Similar | Similar |

## Verdict

The BlendJet 2 wins on battery life and portability. The NutriBullet Go wins on capacity. Choose based on your typical blend size.
