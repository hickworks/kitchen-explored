## Ninja BN801

The Ninja BN801 is a high-performance blender that has the ability to transform the way meals are prepared. It is equipped with multifunctional capabilities with cutting-edge technology. It simplifies and elevates blending tasks to a higher level. One of the standout features of this appliance is its capability to blend and crush ingredients. This has been made possible with the help of a 1400-watt motor, capable of blending and crushing tougher ingredients with ease. This allows you to create your smoothies, frozen drinks and more with minimal effort.

Apart from its blending prowess, the Ninja BN801 also handles food processing well. The appliance comes with 8 8-cup precision bowl which provides precise food processing, for chopping and smoothing purees. This feature is useful because it allows the preparation of a variety of ingredients as well as producing the desired consistency of recipes.

Additionally, the Ninja N801 is equipped with 5 versatile functions which are smoothies, frozen drinks, nutrient extraction, processor bowl and chopped mixtures. The other feature that makes this blender stand out from there others is its Auto-IQ-technology. This technology has the ability to carry out tasks for you preventing guesswork operations. Some of the programs it carries for you are timed pulsing, blending and also pausing patterns.

Furthermore, the blender also comes with 2 24-ounce single-serve cups that are equipped with lids, allowing you to enjoy on-the-go convenience. The other amazing feature is its ability to dough in seconds. This will save time and effort in preparing meals in the kitchen.

Finally, the Ninja BN801 has a 72-ounce pitcher container which is great for blending large batches. So, if you have a large family or hosting a number of guests, this appliance can do it all for you. As we complete, the appliance comes with different blades to handle different tasks, such as dough blade, chopping blade, stacked blade and pro extractor blades. This makes it suitable for different occasions.

## Differences

-   **Power –** The Ninja BN801 is equipped with a 1200-watt motor while the Ninja BN805A has a 1400-watt motor. This shows that the BN805A has more capabilities to blend tougher ingredients with more ease than the BN801.
-   **Versatile functions –** The Ninja BN801 is equipped with 5 versatile functions which are smoothies, nutrient extraction and more. On the other hand, the BN805A does not have any versatile functions. This means that the BN805A is more versatile than the BN801.
-   **Auto-IQ technology** – The Ninja BN801 is constructed with auto-IQ technology, a feature that prevents any guesswork operations by providing you with timed pulsing, blending and pausing patterns. On the other side, the BN805A is not equipped with this feature.
-   **Dough mixer –** The Ninja BN801 is equipped with a powerful dough mixer that can mix dough in seconds. On the other side, the BN805A lacks this feature.
-   **Price -** The Ninja BN801 has a lower price tag of $169.99 while the BN805A has a higher price tag of $215.99. This shows us the BN805A is more expensive than the BN805.
-   **Weight –** The BN805A has a heavier weight of 17.61 pounds while the BN801 has a lighter weight of 8.8 Pounds.
-   **Dimensions –** The BN801 has a dimension of 7.38"D x 9.63" W x 18"H while the BN805A has 17.7 x 15 x 11.6 inches.
