## Overview

All-Clad's D3 and D5 are both professional-grade stainless cookware but use different clad constructions that affect heat distribution and warping resistance.

## Key Differences

| Feature | D3 | D5 |
|---|---|---|
| Layers | 3 | 5 |
| Heat distribution | Even | Very even |
| Warp resistance | Good | Excellent |
| Price | Lower | Higher |

## Verdict

For most home cooks, D3 is more than sufficient and significantly cheaper. D5 shines on induction cooktops where the extra layers reduce hot spots.
