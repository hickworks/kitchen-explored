## Can you grind cinnamon sticks in a blender?

Can you grind cinnamon sticks in a blender?

**Yes, you can grind cinnamon sticks in a blender. However, not all blenders are capable of effectively grinding cinnamon sticks. If your blender has a strong motor and sharp blades, you should be able to pulse the cinnamon sticks a few times to break them up into a fine powder. Be careful not to overdo it, as blending the cinnamon for too long can cause it to become sticky and clump together.**

If your blender is not strong enough to grind cinnamon sticks, you can use a spice grinder or a mortar and pestle to grind the cinnamon into a fine powder.

## How to Grind Cinnamon Sticks in a Blender

To grind cinnamon sticks in a blender, follow these steps:

1.  Break the cinnamon sticks into smaller pieces to make them easier to grind. You can do this by hand or with a knife.
2.  Place the cinnamon pieces into the blender.
3.  Pulse the blender a few times to break up the cinnamon into a fine powder. Be careful not to overdo it, as blending the cinnamon for too long can cause it to become sticky and clump together or cook.
4.  If you want a finer powder, you can sift the ground cinnamon through a fine-mesh sieve to remove any large pieces or clumps.
5.  Once the cinnamon is ground to your desired consistency, you can use it in your recipe or store it in an airtight container for future use.

It's important to note that not all blenders are capable of grinding cinnamon sticks. If your blender doesn't have a strong motor or sharp blades, it may not be able to effectively grind the cinnamon. In this case, it's best to use a spice grinder or mortar and pestle to grind the cinnamon into a fine powder.
