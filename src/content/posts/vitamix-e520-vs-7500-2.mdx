## Vitamix E520

The Vitamix E520 is a powerful, durable and versatile blender that is well-designed to serve the needs of home cooks as well as professional chefs. The blender features a 2.2 horsepower motor that has the capability to pulverize and even blend the toughest ingredients with ease. The blender is suitable for crushing ice, blending frozen fruits, grinding and more.

The other amazing feature is its sleek and modern appearance. Its appearance has the ability to make your kitchen counter space look great. It is also constructed with a 64-ounce tritan pitcher which is great for large batch operation. Additionally, it is also BPA free which makes it safe to use it in preparing meals and drinks. Its size also makes it suitable for small kitchen countertops.

The blender also comes with a tamper tool that helps or allows you to push ingredients toward the blade during operations. This helps prevents any accidents on your hands. To add on top of this, the blender also comes with 10 variable speed settings and pulse functions. The variable settings will allow you to have control over your blend speeds while the pulse will give you full control over textures.

Just on the same line, the short bursts of the pulse feature will enable you to chop vegetables and create chunk salsas easily. These 2 features will enable you to achieve your desired texture and consistency. In addition to this, the blender is equipped with 3 program settings which are smoothies, hot soups and lastly frozen desserts.

Cleaning the blender is an easy task for any because it is built with dishwasher-safe parts. Finally, the blender is versatile and can be used in a wide range of blending tasks. Its usage is easy which makes it suitable for both a beginner and a professional level user. This feature above makes the Vitamix e520 one of the top high-performance blenders on the market.

The blender includes:

-   Motor Base
-   Low-Profile 64-ounce Container
-   2 Chilly Tumbler Cups with Straw
-   Low-Profile Tamper
-   First Blends Cookbook

### **Pros**

-   The Vitamix E520 is versatile.
-   The blender is also powerful.
-   Its high-quality materials enhance its durability.
-   The blender is easy to use making it suitable for beginner to professional users.

### **Cons**
