## Vitamix V1200

The Vitamix V1200 is a high-performance blender that is equipped with a number of impressive features that makes it a popular choice among blender enthusiasts. The blender has a powerful motor of 1440 watts and 2.2 peak horsepower. This motor has the ability to blend tougher ingredients effortlessly. This means that you will be able to blend frozen fruits, crush ice, blend vegetables and more with ease.

The other standout feature is the smart technology integration. Here the blender is equipped with wireless connectivity whereby you can connect the blender to the Vitamix Perfect blend App using your smart device. The app has a number of recipes and also allows you to easily access blend settings to create recipes without guessing. This ensures that the blends delivered are consistent all the time.

In addition to this, the Vitamix V1200 comes with 5 programmable settings which enhances your blending operations. These programs are smoothies, hot soups, dips and frozen spreads, frozen fruits and self-cleaning. With a touch of a button, you will be able to select the applicable program to use. Self-detect containers on the other hand can automatically adjust blending time and speed depending on the container used.

The other feature that comes with the blender is the 10 variable speed settings. These settings allow you to easily choose the speeds of your blending operations. This means that you will have the flexibility of achieving your desired textures as well as consistencies. Whether you want to achieve chunky salsa or velvety puree, this feature will sort you well.

The V1200 is also equipped with a pulse function, this feature enables easier customization of blend textures. When pulsing at high speeds and using short bursts, you will be able to achieve your desired textures quickly and also precisely. This feature can also be useful if you want to distribute ingredients evenly without having to blend them fully.

Cleaning the blender is also not an issue at all. This is because the blender features a cleaning program that reduces the effort for cleaning as well as time. To add on top of this, the blender also comes with a digital timer which assists you to monitor blend times thereby preventing guesswork. The pitcher size on the other hand is a 64-ounce pitcher which is suitable for medium and large batch operations.

The other notable feature of this blender is its hardened stainless-steel blade. This blade is strong and sturdy allowing it to carry out all operations including tougher ones without any issues. Cooling motor on the other hand prevents the motor from overheating when operating continuously or when carrying out tougher operations.
