## Similarities between Vitamix VM0103 vs 5200

-   **Variable speed settings –** Both models have 10 variable speed controls. As we have discussed, this feature gives you full control to use precise blend speeds, in order to achieve your desired textures as well as consistency.
-   **Container size –** The Vitamix VM0103 and 5200 are all equipped with a 64-ounce container. You will be able to carry out medium and large batch blend tasks.
-   **Blades –** Both blenders are equipped with hardened stainless-steel blades they are strong and sturdy to handle tough operations with ease.
-   **Self-cleaning feature –** Both blender models are built with a self-cleaning feature which makes cleaning easy. All you need is soap warm water and run the feature for 30 to 60 seconds.
-   **Dishwasher safe** – Both appliances have dishwasher safe parts which also makes it easy to clean.
-   **Warranty** – These 2 blenders come with a warranty of 7 years. This builds confidence in the quality of the product.
-   **Versatility** – Both models are versatile since you will be able to create a wide range of recipes, such as smoothies, desserts, baby food and more.
-   **Tamper tool –** A tamper tool is included in both models. This ensures a uniform texture and also prevents accidents that would be caused by the blade.
-   **Electrical rating –** Both models have an electrical rating of 120.
