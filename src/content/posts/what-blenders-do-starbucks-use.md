## Maintenance and Cleaning of Starbucks Blenders

1.  **Daily Cleaning** – Starbucks workers clean their blenders after each task so that they are able to prevent residue from building up which can affect the flavor of drinks.
2.  **Deep cleaning** – After daily operations, Starbucks workers disassemble blender components and wash them as well as sanitize them to kill any bacteria. This prevents contamination and also ensures the blender components function well.
3.  **Calibration** \-They calibrate their blenders so that blender mix drinks with the correct consistency. If it is not good, it is taken for recalibration to maintain the quality of the drinks.
4.  **Regular maintenance** – Starbucks maintain their blender regularly to ensure they function well.

## Benefits of regular maintenance and cleaning of blenders

-   **Consistency –** A well-maintained blender leads to even blending or consistent results. One which is not maintained may lead to inconsistent results.
-   **Hygiene –** If a blender is not cleaned well, there is a high possibility it may get bacteria which can lead to food poisoning. Regular cleaning acts as a disinfectant and also prevents the piling of bacteria.
-   **Prolonged lifespan –** A well-maintained blender can last for a long time.
-   **No downtime** – A well-functioning blender does not experience breaks downs like one that is not well-maintained.
