## Is blender bottle dishwasher safe?

Would you like to know whether blender bottles are dishwasher safe? If you are a person who loves protein shakes, smoothies and any other drink, am pretty sure you are familiar with blender bottles. They are innovative containers that are very popular with athletes, fitness enthusiasts and more. They are highly loved because they are convenient to carry around. Reading this article will enable you to get the right answer regarding this topic.

**Yes, most blender bottles are dishwasher safe. However, it is very important to check the manufactures manual or instructions to check whether the bottle is dishwasher safe. This is because some bottles may not be dishwasher safe hence they may get damaged when washed in the dishwasher. To add on top, when cleaning blender bottles in a dishwasher, some components are supposed to be removed such as blender ball and more.**

## Process of cleaning Blender bottles in a dishwasher

1.  **Disassemble** – The first process is to disassemble. The process is easy because all you have to do is remove the lid, flip cap and any other removable component like the blender ball. This will allow for a thorough cleaning process and also prevent any parts from getting damaged during the cleaning cycle.
2.  **Pre-rinse** – This process involves removing any residue or debris in and on the blender bottle. This step allows for a smooth and convenient cleaning operation.
3.  **Placement** – Once you are done rinsing, place the blender bottle in the dishwasher. Ensure you place the bottle on the rack since this will prevent or protect it from direct exposure to high heat. High heat can damage or even affect the durability of the bottle.
4.  **Dishwasher** **settings** – Once everything is done, ensure you set your dishwasher to a gentle cycle that has a low or medium temperature. As we have said above, high heat or heavy-duty cycles can easily damage the blender bottles. To add on top of this, use a detergent that works great with your blender bottle. If you are not sure you can refer to the manufacturer’s manual.
5.  **Components** – The disassembled components can be washed separately or in a dishwasher.
6.  **Drying** – Once the washing cycle is complete, remove the blender bottle and allow it to dry completely. When dry, you can reassemble the parts before using or store. A dry blender bottle will not experience a build of moisture, bacterial growth and odors.

## **Non-Dishwasher-Safe** **Blender Bottles**

We would like you to see why some blender bottles are not dishwasher safe. Here are some of the reasons:

-   **Material Sensitivity** – Some or a few blender bottles don’t have the ability to withstand high temperatures and also agitation from a dishwasher. This is because the bottles can warp, discolor and even degrade or get damaged when exposed to high temperatures. For this reason, handwashing them is the best option.
-   **Lid and seal** – Some blender bottles have lids and seals that are susceptible to damage when washed in a dishwasher. These components are constructed with rubber or silicone which can easily get damaged when exposed to the high temperatures of a dishwasher. This can affect their functionality which may make them leak.
-   **Removable** components – Blender bottles that are equipped with removable parts such as mixing balls (also known as blender balls) require maximum attention when carrying out washing processes. Such parts can be misplaced, dislodge and even damaged during washing cycles in the dishwasher. To avoid complications, handwashing is highly recommended.
-   **Design of blender bottles** – Some blenders are equipped with intricate shapes and crevices that can easily trap food or detergent. This makes the dishwasher unable to reach certain areas. Handwash will be applicable since you easily be able to reach areas where the dishwasher can’t reach thereby preventing bacteria and odor build-up.
-   **Appearance and durability** – Blender bottles are dishwasher safe, however exposing them to the heat of the dishwasher regularly can affect their durability considerably. That is the reason the bottles will fade in color and even lose strength.
-   **Manufacturers recommendations** -Manufacturers' guidelines play a huge role. Some manufacturers clearly state that their blender bottles should not be washed in a dishwasher to retain their optimal performance as well as maintain their durability.

## Alternatives to a dishwasher

There are other methods that you can use as an alternative to clean your blender bottle effectively:

-   **Handwash** – This is one of the main alternatives to a dishwasher. The process is convenient and easy to apply. This process enables you to reach the delicate areas of the bottle that a dishwasher can’t reach.
-   **Soaking** – This is great for stubborn stains and odors. All you have to do is soak the blender bottle in warm water and a baking soda solution or diluted vinegar can also work. Soak it for some hours before washing and rinsing the bottle. This will clean the blender bottle.
-   **Cleaning tablets and solutions** – This is also another way to clean blender bottles. Cleaning tablets and solutions are manufactured to remove stains, odor and also residue. It is good to follow manufacturers' instructions when using this method.
