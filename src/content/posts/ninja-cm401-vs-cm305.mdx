## [Ninja CM401](https://www.amazon.com/Ninja-Specialty-Fold-Away-Frother-Renewed/dp/B07TKNHX29?crid=3HF3FDEI0WKSD&keywords=ninja+cm401&qid=1690580431&sprefix=Ninja+CM401%2Caps%2C1692&sr=8-2&linkCode=sl1&tag=ke2024-20&linkId=4a764098bd6841979150a20379ff9c84&language=en_US&ref_=as_li_ss_tl)

The Ninja CM401 is a great coffee maker that is versatile. This coffee maker features 4 versatile brew styles which are classic, rich, over ice and specialty brew. Having this feature gives you the joy of brewing super-rich concentrate coffee suitable for lattes, macchiatos and more.

The Ninja CM401 also allows you to easily brew fresh coffee over ice. The coffee result produced is a well-flavored iced coffee that is perfect and will not water down. Worried about sizes, worry not because this appliance comes with 6 different brewing sizes allowing flexibility. You can opt for a single cup, half or even a full carafe. Some of the options you can use are cups, xl cup, travel mug and more. You will use the right amount for the right occasion,

Unlike some coffee makers on the market that require the use of single-use pods. The Ninja CM401 enables you to make your favorite ground without the use of reusable pods. This makes it convenient and eco-friendly for the environment. The Built in fold-away frother on the other hand make it easy for you to create creamy and smooth frothy coffee. Whether you want the coffee hot or cold, this feature will do it effortlessly. It is also removable which makes cleaning a breeze.

The other notable feature is the 40-ounce removable water reservoir that is easy to refill. For those who don’t like cleaning, we would like to inform you that the process of cleaning is very simple and effortless. This is because this coffee maker has removable parts such as a filter holder, flip-top lid and more. To add on top the parts are dishwasher safe making it save time and energy for cleaning.

The safety of the appliance is a priority because it is equipped with features such as auto shut-off. With this feature, you will have peace of mind because the appliance shuts down itself if it is inactive for 1 hour. Apart from safety, it will also save energy consumption.

## Differences between Ninja cm401 vs cm305

The following are the differences between the Ninja CM401 and CM305:

-   **Brew styles** – The Ninja CM401 has 4 brewing styles which are classic, rich, over-ice and specialty brew. On the other hand, the CM305 has 3 brewing styles which are classic, rich and over-ice.  The CM305 does not have a specialty brew style. This means that the CM401 is more versatile than CM305.
-   **Specialty brew** – The Ninja CM401 has the capability to carry specialty brew while the CM305 does not have this feature.
-   **Removable** water reservoir – The CM305 has a removable water reservoir equipped with a metering while, the CM401 water reservoir does not have an auto metering feature.
-   **Thermal extraction technology** – The CM305 is equipped with the thermal technology feature while the Ninja CM401 does not have this feature.
-   **Frother** – The CM401 has an inbuilt frother while the CM305.This feature plays a huge role in changing hot or cold milk to achieve a silky smooth froth.
-   **24-hour delay-brew** – The Ninja 305 is built with a 24-hour delay brew while the CM401 does not have this feature.
-   **Price** – The cost of purchasing the CM401 is higher than the CM305. The CM401 has a price tag of $169.95 while the CM305 has a price tag of $145.00.
-   **Noise level** – The noise level produced by the Ninja CM401 is lower than that produced by CM305.
-   **Weight** \- The weight of CM 305 is 9.18 Pounds while CM401 has 3.1 Pounds. This means that the CM401 is lighter than the CM305.
-   **Dimensions** – The CM401 has a dimension of 8.75"D x 11.99" W x 15.04"H while the CM305 has 15 Inches (H) x 8.94 Inches (W) x 12.2 Inches (D).
