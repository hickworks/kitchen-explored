## How to make pesto in a blender

The process of making pesto in a blender is very simple and straightforward. Before you begin the operation you will require the following ingredients:

-   2 cups of fresh basil leaves
-   ½ a cup of pine nuts
-   ½ a cup of parmesan cheese
-   3 cloves of garlic
-   ½ a cup of virgin olive oil
-   Salt
-   Pepper

-   **Step 1**

**Toast pine nuts –** The first step is to heat a small portion of the skillet with medium heat and add the pine nuts. Toast the pine nuts for a few minutes until they change their color to brown. It is important to monitor them since they can burn quickly. Once you are done, remove the nuts from the heat and allow them to cool.

-   **Step 2**

**Ingredients preparations –** The next step is to peel garlic clove. You can also use this step to grate the parmesan cheese.

-   **Step 3**

**Blend ingredients –** At this step take all the ingredients and place them into the blender container for blending. By the term all ingredients I mean basil leaves, garlic cloves, grated parmesan cheese and salt. Pepper and toasted pine. To blend with a low speed and increase the speed gradually to medium-high.

-   **Step 4**

**Add olive oil –** Add olive oil slowly while the blend is still running. This helps in emulsification which produces a smooth consistency. Continue the blending process until you achieve a uniform or well-combined texture of your preferences.

-   **Step 5**

**Seasoning** – Before you season, taste the pesto. This will enable you to know the amount of salt and pepper to add at this stage. You can also add any other seasonings of your desire. Once you have added salt and pepper, blend for a few seconds or minutes to incorporate the seasoning with the pesto.

-   **Step 6**

**Serve or store –** Once you are done blending, stop the blender and remove the pitcher container. You can use the pesto directly from here or store. For storage, put it in an airtight container and put it in a refrigerator. It can stay in a refrigerator for up to 7 days.

If you follow this step correctly, I am very that you will be able to make pesto using a blender successfully.

## Uses of pesto

Pesto is versatile and can be used in many ways. The following are some of the uses of pesto:

-   **Pasta sauce** – Pesto can be used as a pasta sauce in some pasta dishes. Pesto has the ability to add flavor and aromatic elements to your dishes.
-   **Sandwich bread** – You can also use pesto as a spread and wrap for sandwiches.
-   **Pizza topping** – Pesto can also be used as a pizza topping. It can be an alternative to tomato sauce.
-   **Salad dressing** – Yes, it can also be used as a salad dressing together with olive oil or vinegar to produce a flavorful salad dressing.
-   **Marinde** – It is also used as a marinade for meats such as chicken or fish.
-   **Dip –** Pesto has the ability to add flavor when used as a dip for vegetables, bread and more.
-   **Soup garnish** – It can also be used as a garnish soup for tomato and more.
