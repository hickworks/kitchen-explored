## Corelle vs Mikasa Dinnerware: Which Is the Better Choice?

Whether you are looking to purchase dinnerware for yourself or even gift someone, selecting the perfect dinnerware can be quite a hard task. This guide will compare the corelle and mikasa dinnerware. We will discuss their features, pros, cons, similarities as well as differences. This will guide will help you choose one that suits your needs.

**The difference between Corelle and Mikasa is the material construction. Both dinnerware sets are built with high-quality materials that are designed to last for a long time. However, Corelle is more durable than Mikasa because it is constructed with vitrelle material and multiple layers of glass fused together. On the other hand, Mikasa is less durable because it is constructed from a mix of minerals and traditional clay such as kaolin, quartz and feldspar.**

## Corelle

Corelle is a popular brand that deals in manufacturing high-quality, lightweight and durable dinnerware sets. They offer a wide variety of designs as well as patterns to choose from. This means that you will have a set for every occasion, from casual dinnerware sets to formal dinnerware sets. Corelle is constructed from Vitrelle and glass material which is a strong and durable material that is highly resistant to chipping or breaking.

**Types of Corelle Dishes**

Corelle dinnerware has a variety of dishes that can be used for different tasks. The following are some of the Corelle categories of dishes:

-   **Corelle livingware: This is** Corelle's popular collection. It is highly loved because of its design as well as durability. The collection ranges from casual to formal dinnerware sets.
-   **Corelle Impression**: This collection consists of dinnerware sets that are equipped with unique patterns and designs that have vibrant colors.
-   **Corelle square**: This type of set is constructed with a square shape and a modern elegant design.
-   **Corelle Boutique**: This is Corelle's premium collection that consists of exquisite designs as well as patterns that have an elegant touch.
-   **Corelle Vitelle glass**: This category has lightweight, durable and also chip-resistant materials that are required in the build-up of Corelle dinnerware sets.

### Pros of Corelle Dishes

The following are the pros of Corelle dishes:

-   Corelle dinnerware sets are durable.
-   The dinnerware sets are lightweight which makes it easy to handle them.
-   They also save on space because they can be stacked together.
-   The sets are versatile and come in a wide variety to serve different occasions.
-   Cleaning the sets is also easy because they are dishwasher safe.
-   The dishes are oven safe.
-   The purchasing price is affordable.
-   Come in a wide variety of designs, sizes and colors to choose from.

### **Cons of Corelle Dishes**

-   They are not suitable to be used on broilers or stovetops.

## Maintenance and Cleaning Tips for Corelle Mikasa

1.  Do not use abrasive materials or steel since they can scratch or damage the surface of the dinnerware sets. Instead, use a sponge or bristled brush.
2.  Handwash gently and if you use a dishwasher, use the gentle cycle to prevent chipping or breaking.
3.  For stubborn stains, you can use baking soda and water to remove the stains by scrubbing.
4.  When storing, avoid stacking, or do it carefully if possible because it can cause cracks or chips on the plates.
5.  Do not store the plates when they are wet because they can cause warping, water spot, or moisture residue.
6.  To be on the safe side, refer to the manufacturer's manual or instructions when using the sets
