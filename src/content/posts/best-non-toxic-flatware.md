## Non Toxic Stainless Steel Flatware

Would you like to know the best Non toxic flatware? Non toxic flatware is increasing in popularity because people are realizing the dangers of using toxins flatware. Flatware or cutlery refers to utensils used for eating, serving and cutting. Good example of flatware is knives, forks, spoons and more, it can either be plastic or made from metal.

Unfortunately, a variety of flatware available on the market contains toxic chemicals that may be dangerous to the human body. These chemicals may leach into your food when heated up or may react to acidic foods such as citrus fruits. This is where non-toxic flatware steps in because it eliminates and also prevents potential health risks by providing free chemical flatware.

Non toxic flatware is made out of stainless steel that has been tested to eliminate chemicals such as lead and other heavy metals that easily be found in traditional flatware. Luckily, the majority of this flatware are dishwasher safe which makes it very easy to clean. To add on top, most of the, are equipped with an ergonomic design that can enhance the look of any table setting.

## Types of non toxic flatware

The following are some of the non toxic flatware available on the market:

1.  **Stainless steel** – Stainless steel is one of the most popular non-toxic flatware. It is highly known for its durability as well as affordability. It does not contain any toxic substances such as lead, cadmium and more. In addition, the sets are very easy to clean and they won’t corrode over time like other flatware available on the market.
2.  **Bamboo flatware** – Bambo is another flatware that is increasing in popularity at a higher rate. This amazing flatware is BPA-free and also has the capability of withstanding high temperatures without leaching any chemicals into your food.
3.  **Silicone** – Silicon is also a great non toxic flatware. They are very popular because of their flexibility, lightweight and more durable than other flatware. Though they are very expensive than traditional flatware, they are much safer to consume food directly since they don’t contain any fumes or chemicals that may be hazardous to your health. This makes a suitable and safe choice for those who want eco-friendly flatware.

**Frequently Asked Question**

**Is non-toxic flatware dishwasher safe?**

Most non-toxic flatware are dishwasher safe. However, it is highly recommended to check the manufacturer's manual so that you are very sure that the flatware is dishwasher safe. A few are not and may get damaged when cleaned in a dishwasher.

**Can non-toxic flatware be used for outdoor dining?**

Yes, nontoxic flatware can be used in outdoor dining. The non-toxic flatware is durable, resistant to corrosion and also lightweight. These features make it perfect for outdoor usage.
