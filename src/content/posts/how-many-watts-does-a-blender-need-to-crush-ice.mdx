## What is a blender and what do they do?

A blender is a popular kitchen appliance that is used to blend, grind chop and mix different ingredients which creates smooth, textured as well as volumetric textures. The market has a wide variety of blender brands, which come in different sizes, prices, power as well as functionality. The 2 common blenders are the immersion blender and the countertop blender.

The immersion and countertop blender can be used for a variety of blending tasks, from crushing ice to creating smoothies and more. Choosing the right blender plays a huge role when it comes to crushing ice.

When choosing a blender that can crush ice, it's very important to consider the power of the motor. A blender with a powerful motor has the ability to crush the ice with ease, while a blender with a less powerful motor may find it very difficult to crush the ice, which may lead to unappealing results.

If you are planning on acquiring a blender that can crush ice easily, then acquiring a motor that has 600 watts and more will be a better option. Blenders that have 600 watts and more also have powerful blades that can crush or blend tougher ingredients like ice with ease. Blenders with 600 watts and blow tend to have a hard time crushing ice and the results may not be great as a 600 watts motor and more.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Can you use a food processor to blend ice cubes?

A food processor can be used to blend ice cubes too, but it is not as effective as a dedicated ice crusher or blender. The blades of a food processor are not designed to carry out crush ice operations and will tend to struggle. If you do use a food processor to blend ice cubes, you'll likely need to add more water than usual to help the blades move well and prevent them from getting damaged.
