## Vitamix A3500 vs V1200

Would you like to know the difference between the Vitamix V1200 and A3500? When it comes to choosing one of the 2, the Vitamix V1200 and A3500, it can be a daunting task. These 2 appliances perform exceptionally well when it comes to blending. In this guide, we will go head-to-head comparison. This will include features, similarities, differences, pros and cons of each appliance.

Let’s get started,

**The main difference between the Vitamix V1200 and A3500 is the program settings feature. The Vitamix A3500 has 5 program settings which are smoothies, hot soups, frozen desserts, dips & Spreads and self-cleaning functions. On the other hand, the V1200 lacks this feature. This makes the A3500 more versatile and convenient to carry blending tasks than the V1200.**
