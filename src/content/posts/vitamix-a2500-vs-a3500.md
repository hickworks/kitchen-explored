## Overview

Both the A2500 and A3500 belong to Vitamix's Ascent series and share the same powerful motor and wireless connectivity. The main differences come down to controls and price.

## Key Differences

| Feature | A2500 | A3500 |
|---|---|---|
| Controls | 3 program settings | 5 program settings |
| Display | LED | Touchscreen |
| Built-in timer | No | Yes |
| Price | Lower | Higher |

## Verdict

The A2500 is the sweet spot for most home blenders. The A3500's touchscreen and timer are nice but not essential. Save the money unless you blend daily.
