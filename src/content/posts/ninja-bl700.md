## **Tips for using a blender**

There are a few tips to keep in mind when using a blender, especially a professional one like the Ninja Bl700.

First, always add a little liquid to the blender before putting any solid foods. This will act as a lubricant help the blades move more easily and also prevent clogging.

Second, ensure to start with smaller pieces of food and gradually add larger ones. This prevents clogging.

Finally, avoid overfilling the blender. Ensure that you leave some room at the top so that the ingredients can circulate easily.

These tips will help you get the most out of your Ninja Blender and make delicious smoothies, soups, sauces and more.
