## Ninja CO401b

The Ninja CO401B blender can enhance your food processing tasks to another level. The appliance has a well-designed processing lid equipped with a feed chute and a select cut processing disc. The processing lid enables you to have precise shredding, slicing and even grating. This means you will have an easier time creating salads, stir-fry, desserts and more.

The other stand-out feature is the 1600-watt motor. This is very powerful enough to process any kind of food with ease. Additionally, it is equipped with variable speed control and smart torque technology.  Such features allow the appliance to power through any ingredients without facing any issues. The results produced are also smooth and consistent.

For smoothie lovers, the appliance comes with an XL smoothie bowl and a built-in tamper to help you acquire consistent and uniform results. To add on top, the amount of liquid required is little enabling you to get a thick and creamy smoothie.The XL smoothie can also enhance your game when experimenting with different textures as well as flavors.

In addition, the Ninja CO401b comes with 7 preset Auto IQ programs. The 7 programs are smoothies, extraction, bowls, spreads, food processing, dough and chopping. This feature prevents guesswork operations when creating smoothies, extracting nutrients and more. When you choose the program you want, the appliance will do the work for you. To add on top, it is user-friendly and convenient to use, saving you time in the kitchen.

The appliance comes with:

-   A 1600 peak watt motor
-   72-ounce processor pitcher
-   Feed chute lid equipped with a 2-part pusher
-   18-ounce XL smoothie bowl maker equipped with a tamper as well as a storage lid
-   24-ounce Nutrient extraction cup
-   Total crushing and chopping blade
-   Shredding disc
-   Grating disc
-   Dough blade
-   Hybrid Edge blade assembly

### **Pros**

-   It has a friendly user interface that is easy to use.
-   The appliance is powerful, thereby carrying out tasks effectively.
-   Capable of handling large batch operations with ease.
-   It is also versatile.

### **Cons**

-   Its price is relatively high compared to other blenders on the market.

## Ninja SS401

The Ninja SS401 is another appliance that can elevate your food processing tasks to a higher level. Just like the Ninja CO401b, this one also comes with a feed chute and select cutting process discs. Discs that allow you to experience precise slicing, grating and more. In addition to this, you will have the freedom to create many dishes like salads, desserts and more.

One of the notable features is its 1600-peak-watt motor and smart torque technology that does not stall at all. This means that you don’t have to worry even when blending the toughest ingredients. For those who enjoy smoothies, the appliance is up to the task. This is because it is equipped with an XL smoothie bowl maker and a tamper tool. It also requires the usage of little liquid to achieve a thick and creamy smoothie.

Additionally, the SS401 also comes with a Nutrient extraction cup that is great for providing an efficient breakdown of ingredients. The cup ensures that out extract vitamins and nutrients maximumly. Variable speed control settings enable you to easily adjust the speed and texture for blend tasks. Whether you want to achieve smooth puree or chunky salsa, you can customize your blends to achieve what you want.

In addition, the SS401 is also constructed with 7 auto IQ programs which enhances convenience. These programs include chopping, food processing, dough, spreads, bowls, smoothies and chopping. All this can be done with a touch of a button preventing guesswork operations. You will also be able to achieve consistent and reliable outcomes all the time. The clean task is also simple because the components are dishwasher safe.

-   The SS401 is also easy to clean.
-   Carries food processing tasks with ease with the help of a 1600-watt motor.
