## Vitamix 750 vs 5200

When it comes to the world of blenders, Vitamix is a trusted brand on the market. Vitamix blenders are highly loved because of their quality, versatility as well as durability. This makes it a popular option for professional chefs as well as home cooks alike. This blog has an in-depth look at these 2 high-performance blenders, the Vitamix 5200 and 750. This will include features, performance and also the overall value of the 2 blenders. This information will help you purchase the blender that suits your needs.

**The main difference between the Vitamix 5200 vs 750 is the horsepower. The Vitamix 750 is equipped with a 2.2 horsepower motor while the Vitamix 5200 has a 2.0 horsepower motor. This shows us that the Vitamix 750 is more powerful and has the ability to handle tougher blend operations with ease compared to the Vitamix 5200.**
